Practical knowledge of language is learnt at-

(A) ** School

(B) Language laboratory

(C) Language teaching

(D) Language instruction

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Practical knowledge includes math, science, and technology skills that are needed in everyday life. It can be learned through hands-on activities such as building something or exploring how things work.

Concept note-2: -Practical Learning is achieved through doing things and is based on real-life undertakings and tasks. If we take cooking as an example, instead of learning about cooking through books and blogs, actually put your culinary skills to the test and cook a dish.

Concept note-3: -Practical knowledge is when a person gets some sort of hands-on experience with the concept under discussion. He/she gets to work on the concept, and understand by experience more than reading, why some method is used, what problems arise while following the method, and how to rectify it.

Concept note-4: -Take training courses. You can improve business skills by taking a training course relevant to your field. Practise effective communication. Be open to feedback. Be proactive. Set milestones. 02-May-2022
