TEACHING APTITUDE IN EDUCATION

[SOURCES]
At authoritarian level teaching is.

(A) ** Teacher - centered

(B) Child - centered

(C) Headmaster centered

(D) Experience - based

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It is a teacher-centred method as the teacher has full control of the class. The activity is planned according to the teacher. The learner has less chance to express themselves.

Concept note-2: -The authoritarian classroom management style is basically a style where the teacher has complete control over the classroom. Students are not actively involved or responsive. In this strictest form of class management style, it is quite likely that a student not following the set rules can be punished.

Concept note-3: -(i) Autocratic or Authoritarian teacher: Autocratic teaching is a traditional style of teaching. These are teacher-centered or content centred. In this method, the teacher has full control over students and teaching. Here, the teacher is more active and learners are passive listeners.

Concept note-4: -The authoritative style is characterized by behavioral principles, high expectations of appropriate behavior, clear statements about why certain behaviors are acceptable and others not acceptable, and warm student-teacher relationships.

Concept note-5: -Authoritarian teachers create a highly-structured learning environment with a strong emphasis on the rules. They expect undisputed compliance from their students and do not tolerate inappropriate behavior in the classroom.
