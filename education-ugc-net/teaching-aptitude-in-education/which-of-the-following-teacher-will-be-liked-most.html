Which of the following teacher, will be liked most?

(A) ** A loving teacher

(B) A teacher who is disciplined

(C) A teacher of high idealistic attitude

(D) A teacher who often amuses his students

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hence, a loving teacher will be liked most by her students because a disciplinarian teacher or one with a high idealistic attitude is likely to unsettle the average students. A teacher who amuses in class risks being taken lightly by the overenthusiastic and audacious students.

Concept note-2: -Thus, compassionate teachers, are most liked by the students.

Concept note-3: -A teacher of love always respects the person. The love instructor is kind and understanding. The love teacher ignores the shortcomings of a person, and views the person as a whole. The educator imparts affection, even if the person doesnâ€™t understand or appreciates that.

Concept note-4: -Learners want their teacher to be democratic and the one who treats all of them equally by providing equal opportunities for learning to them. The teacher should be fair regarding the learning opportunities, teaching methods, and while evaluating the performance of learners.

Concept note-5: -Some qualities of a good teacher include skills in communication, listening, collaboration, adaptability, empathy and patience. Other characteristics of effective teaching include an engaging classroom presence, value in real-world learning, exchange of best practices and a lifelong love of learning.
