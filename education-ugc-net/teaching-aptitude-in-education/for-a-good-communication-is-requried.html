For a good communication …..is requried.

(A) ** Clarity of thought

(B) Dramatic Presentation

(C) Speaking in a mild tone

(D) Speaking without pause

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Clarity and simplicity is the most critical component of effective communication. Its presence leads to accurate understanding, greater efficiency, and higher engagement of those involved; its absence results in chaos and loss of precious time and resources.

Concept note-2: -Content, clarity of thought, having an interesting perspective and belief in the idea are the foundation for effective communication. If these ingredients are missing, then it’s hard to communicate and influence people.

Concept note-3: -Clarity-Clarity implies emphasizing on a specific message or goal at a time, rather than trying to achieve too much at once. Clarity in communication has following features: It makes understanding easier. Complete clarity of thoughts and ideas enhances the meaning of message.

Concept note-4: -Every sentence or even paragraph should carry only one central idea. Sentences should never be packed with ideas agreeing or contradicting each other. Sentences should emphasise the main idea. In short, clear writing means use of familiar words or expressions, and avoidance of jargons and unknown acronyms.

Concept note-5: -5 ways to communicate better. Text: Sreeradha D Basu, ET Bureau. Prepare yourself on the subject. Structure your thoughts. Keep it simple. Listen to others with intent. Engage well. 05-Aug-2016
