TEACHING APTITUDE IN EDUCATION

[SOURCES]
The academic performance of students can be improved if parents are encouraged to

(A) arrange for extra tuition

(B) remain unconcerned about it

(C) interact with teachers frequently

(D) ** supervise the work of their wards

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Responsible parents do monitor and supervise the homework of their wards and also help them in learning concepts at home. It is believed that if parents help or supervise the work of their wards, it will encourage the children and also help in attaining the learning goals and improve their academics performance.

Concept note-2: -Finally, with regards to the impact of level of parental involvement on their children’s academic performance, the results of this research found that students with highly involved parents had better academic performance and higher test scores in all the subjects than students with parents who were not involved.

Concept note-3: -Therefore, there is an association between parental motivation and students’ interest in their studies. involved in their children, it will encourage their children to get motivated in achieving their goals and also making them to get more focused in their performance at school [10].
