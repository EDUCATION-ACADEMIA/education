Main responsibility of School Management lies on –

(A) Principal

(B) Teachers

(C) ** Manager

(D) All of the

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hence, we conclude that the main responsibility of School Management lies on principle, teachers, and managers.

Concept note-2: -The responsibilities of such individuals includes tasks like taking care of the funds, making sure that everything runs smoothly, ensuring discipline in the school, ensuring the safety of both the students as well as teachers.

Concept note-3: -The Head of School is responsible for the effective general management of the School, for ensuring the provision of academic leadership and strategic vision, and for the quality of the student experience.

Concept note-4: -Category: Aspects of School Management It involves planning, organizing, actuating, and controlling performance to determine and accomplish set objectives by the use of human beings and other resources.

Concept note-5: -Try direct access to parents. The school administration system must establish direct contact between schools and parents. Make classroom management effective. Build stress-free school management. Take regular feedback. Adopt smart system. 25-Apr-2022
