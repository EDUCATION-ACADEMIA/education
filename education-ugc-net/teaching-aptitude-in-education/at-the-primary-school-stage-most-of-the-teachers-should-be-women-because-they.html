[SOURCES]
At the primary school stage, most of the teachers should be women because they

(A) are available on lower salaries.

(B) can teach children better than men.

(C) know basic content better than men.

(D) ** can deal with children with love and affection.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The most frequently offered explanation by both students (42 %) and teachers (45 %) for the low proportion of men in primary teaching was linked to the perception that it is a woman’s job or that it relates to the mother’s role.

Concept note-2: -Hence, Game teaching is the best technique of teaching at primary stage.

Concept note-3: -A primary teacher is an eternal learner who knows that it’s perfectly okay to make mistakes and try again. This means challenging students to step out of their comfort zones in order to develop a different skillset every day. A well-organised primary teacher values the importance of classroom management skills.

Concept note-4: -Provide Structure. Most kids respond positively to having structure in their classroom. Incorporate Humor into Lessons. Use Student Interests to Your Advantage. Incorporate Story Telling into Lessons. Show an Interest in Their Lives Outside of School. Treat Them With Respect. 05-Jul-2019
