TEACHING APTITUDE IN EDUCATION

[SOURCES]
The essence of an effective classroom environment is

(A) strict discipline

(B) pin-drop silence

(C) a variety of teaching aids

(D) ** lively student-teacher interaction

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The essence of an effective classroom environment is vibrant student-teacher interaction because the classroom feels lively and an environment in which the students can learn can be only created where the students are always actively participating in the teaching-learning process.

Concept note-2: -Classroom interactions assist a kid with learning, explain questions, and take an interest at a subject from a different perspective. Asking questions can lead to better understanding: There is nothing known as silly question. Asking questions to teachers will empower the kid to understand the topic better.

Concept note-3: -A positive classroom environment is one in which students feel comfortable sharing their thoughts, taking risks, asking questions and confronting challenges in their learning.

Concept note-4: -A positive classroom environment helps improve attention, reduce anxiety, and supports emotional and behavioural regulation of students. When educators foster a positive learning culture; learners are more likely to acquire higher motivation that leads to wonderful learning outcomes.

Concept note-5: -Know your students. In addition to their names and experiences, determine their skills and knowledge. Create a welcoming learning environment. Make students feel comfortable and important. Set and communicate expectations. Encourage students to interact positively with one another.
