By which of the following methods the true evaluation of the students is possible?

(A) Formative evaluation

(B) ** Continuous evaluation

(C) Evaluation twice in a year

(D) Evaluation at the end of the course

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -So Continuous evaluation is the method that helps for the true evaluation of the students. Therefore, option 3 is the correct answer.

Concept note-2: -Continuous evaluation should be used for true evaluation of students as continuous evaluation helps in: making reliable decisions about educational planning.

Concept note-3: -Continuous evaluation is the best method to evaluate the achievement of students as it helps in: making reliable decisions about educational planning.

Concept note-4: -Continuous assessment is a form of educational examination that evaluates a student’s progress throughout a prescribed course. It is often used as an alternative to the final examination system.

Concept note-5: -Thus, it is clear that using a variety of ways to assess children over a period of time is the most effective method for the success of Continuous and Comprehensive Evaluation.
