What is the best way for a teacher to resolve the problems in a class?

(A) Ask for other teacher’s opinion

(B) Depends on one’s own opinion

(C) Use the view that Principal gives

(D) ** Think on suggestions offered by the children and implement the good ones

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -By making use of chalk and board method in her teaching. By providing students with diverse materials and encouraging them to explore their materials. By giving them continuous practice questions and laying stress on drill only.

Concept note-2: -Give suggestions not answers. Offer a problem solving heuristic. Teach a variety of problem solving strategies. Allow time for the students to struggle with the problem. Choose problems that require time to think through a solution. Provide a variety of problems. More items

Concept note-3: -Pause to calm down. Be a positive role model. Speak respectfully. Go through the right channels. Avoid defensiveness. Identify the problem. Identify wants, needs and concerns. Come up with possible solutions. More items •10-Sept-2021

Concept note-4: -Keep your cool. Get on their level. Watch your body language. Talk them down. Listen. 04-May-2015
