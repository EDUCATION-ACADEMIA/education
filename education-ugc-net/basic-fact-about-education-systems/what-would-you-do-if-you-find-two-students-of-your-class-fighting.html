What would you do if you find two students of your class fighting?

(A) Ignore the fight

(B) ** Punish both of them

(C) Ignore the aggressor

(D) Punish the aggressive child

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Intervening safely in fights and conflicts Remain calm and do not draw additional student attention to the incident. Get assistance en route to the scene or as soon as possible. Briefly assess the situation, including the participants, the audience, and your surroundings, before jumping into the middle of a crowd.

Concept note-2: -1. The basic rule is to stay out of the fight. Do not intervene. (If your school or district has this as a policy, you could face disciplinary action for trying to break up the fight.

Concept note-3: -report to the headmaster. report to the parents. punish the student appropriately. undertake a case study on the student. 02-Dec-2022

Concept note-4: -Do not allow the behavior to continue. Avoid making it a class issue – address only the student who is causing the disruption. Avoid an argument or shouting match. Do not blame or ridicule the student, or use sarcasm. Do not touch the student.
