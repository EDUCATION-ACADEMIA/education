BASIC FACT ABOUT EDUCATION SYSTEMS

[SOURCES]
EFA Programme is involved in ____

(A) Removing poverty

(B) Removing illiteracy

(C) ** Providing primary education to all

(D) Improving administration of schools

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Education for All movement is a global commitment to provide quality basic education for all children, youth and adults. Education is the key to the new global economy, from primary school on up to life-long learning and it is central to development, social progress and human freedom.

Concept note-2: -The program aims to democratise education by ensuring that children across all economic backgrounds get equal access to quality learning opportunities.

Concept note-3: -The EDI value for a given country is the arithmetic mean of indicators measuring each of its components. It falls between 0 and 1, with 1 representing full EFA achievement across the four goals. The Education for All Global Monitoring Report annually draws on internationally comparable data to calculate the EDI.

Concept note-4: -Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan (SSA) is implemented as Indiaâ€™s main Programme for universalizing elementary education.

Concept note-5: -Education in India is primarily managed by state-run public education system, which fall under the command of the government at three levels: central, state and local.
