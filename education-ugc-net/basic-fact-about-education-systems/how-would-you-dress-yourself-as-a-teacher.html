How would you dress yourself as a teacher?

(A) ** Dignified

(B) Traditionally

(C) Comfortably

(D) Fashionably

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sari is considered moredignified, particularly in south India.” But, there are also those like Calibre Educational Foundation Trustee Madhuvanthi Arun who believe that it is not what one wears, but how one wears it.

Concept note-2: -Pair a dress shirt with dress pants and a blazer. You can also try wearing a brightly colored blazer or top with a dark-colored pair of pants. Wear flats or dress sneakers with this outfit for a comfortable, easy look. Some teachers like wearing a tie or a bowtie with a dress shirt for a more formal option.

Concept note-3: -Dignity means respect for humanity. Teachers must respect every person, regardless of gender, sexual orientation, gender diversity, appearance, age, religion, social standing, origin, opinions, abilities and achievements.

Concept note-4: -Some useful ground rules include: treat each other with dignity and respect. listen to each others’ points of view, recognising that there may be disagreement. keep discussion and comments on the topic, and off the people.
