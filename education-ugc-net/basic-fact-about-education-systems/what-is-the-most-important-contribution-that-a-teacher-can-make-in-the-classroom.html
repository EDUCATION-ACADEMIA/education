What is the most important contribution that a teacher can make in the classroom?

(A) Develop interest in the lesson

(B) Set high achievement levels

(C) Impart knowledge of the subject

(D) ** Reduce tension and make learning creative

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What is the most important contribution that a teacher can make in the classroom? They avoid stagnation at all costs and maintain an desirable passion for children and the learning process.

Concept note-2: -Dedication. One of the most important parts of teaching is having dedication. Teachers not only listen, but also coach and mentor their students. They are able to help shape academic goals and are dedicated to getting their students to achieve them.

Concept note-3: -It is a truism that teachers play a critical role in the development of student creativity. How teachers respond to their students’ ideas, views and suggestions during lessons can be expected to have an influence on the students’ subsequent effort and inclination in coming up with new ideas, views and suggestions.

Concept note-4: -Assess Your Stress Level. Schedule Time to Respond to Your Stress. Establish Realistic Goals. Focus on What You Can Control. Contact Your Colleagues for Advice. Participate in Stress-Relieving Activities. Prioritize Your Health, Family Time, and Quality Sleep. Relax. More items •28-Sept-2020

Concept note-5: -Set time aside for journaling. Participate in five minutes of mindfulness each day. Build brainstorming sessions. Use gamification to encourage participation. Encourage risk taking. Leave the classroom more often. Allow students to teach. Use visual aids. More items •15-Sept-2020
