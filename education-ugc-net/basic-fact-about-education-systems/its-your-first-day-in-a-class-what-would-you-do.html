Its your first day in a class, what would you do?

(A) Smile at everyone

(B) Evaluate the subject

(C) ** Introduce yourself to the students

(D) Ask the students to stay disciplined

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Greet students and introduce yourself once everyone is seated. Include your name (what you would like them to address you by), your academic background, and your interests. You could say something like, “Good morning class, my name is John Smith, you can call me John or Professor Smith.

Concept note-2: -Thanks for giving me this opportunity to introduce myself. My name is [your first name]. I’m from [name of the city] where I finished my schooling last year from [name of the school]. Is there anyone here from my city? (Changes tack to engage with the audience.)

Concept note-3: -Hello, everyone! My name is and I’m from . I’m currently studying at, and I hope to become one day. I’m really passionate about, and I’m always looking for new ways to learn and improve my skills.

Concept note-4: -Create a Plan for Your First Day. Visit a Day Before the First Day (if possible) Reach Early and Welcome Students. Present Everything Confidently. Get to Know Each Other. Introduce Rules and Regulations for Class. Departmental requirements/expectations. More items
