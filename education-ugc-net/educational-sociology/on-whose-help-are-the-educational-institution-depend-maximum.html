On whose help are the educational institution depend maximum?

(A) ** State

(B) Family

(C) Society

(D) Community

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -213. On whose help are the educational institution depend maximum? (d) Family.

Concept note-2: -Education in India is primarily managed by state-run public education system, which fall under the command of the government at three levels: central, state and local.

Concept note-3: -Head of the institution means the Principal of a College / Institution who is responsible for all academic activities of that College / Institution and for implementation of relevant Rules and Regulations.

Concept note-4: -As per the results of a survey across India on higher education, there were around 43 thousand colleges across India in 2021. A majority of the colleges in the country are private unaided colleges.

Concept note-5: -tribunal in civil, taxation and criminal cases; (e) “educational institution” means a school, seminary, college, university, professional academies, training institutes. Central Government Act.
