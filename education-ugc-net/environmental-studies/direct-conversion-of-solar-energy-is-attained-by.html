Direct conversion of solar energy is attained by

(A) Galvanic cells

(B) Electrolytic cells

(C) Hydrogen fuel cells

(D) ** Solar photovoltaic system

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Solar photovoltaic enables the direct conversion of solar energy into electric energy.

Concept note-2: -Photovoltaic cells convert sunlight into electricity A photovoltaic (PV) cell, commonly called a solar cell, is a nonmechanical device that converts sunlight directly into electricity. Some PV cells can convert artificial light into electricity.

Concept note-3: -When the sun shines onto a solar panel, energy from the sunlight is absorbed by the PV cells in the panel. This energy creates electrical charges that move in response to an internal electrical field in the cell, causing electricity to flow.

Concept note-4: -1) Photovoltaic Solar Panels Photovoltaic (PV) solar panels use the sunâ€™s power to create a flow of electricity. This is the most widely adopted method of harvesting solar energy today.

Concept note-5: -Solar PV systems are power systems that convert sunlight into electricity by utilizing the photovoltaic effect. This is a process in which semiconducting materials generate voltage and current when exposed to light.
