First International Earth summit was held at

(A) Germany

(B) ** Stockholm

(C) Johannesburg

(D) Riode Janeiro

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 1972 United Nations Conference on the Environment in Stockholm was the first world conference to make the environment a major issue.

Concept note-2: -In 1992, more than 100 heads of state met in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil for the first international Earth Summit convened to address urgent problems of environmental protection and socio-economic development.

Concept note-3: -The first International Earth Summit was held in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, in 1992.

Concept note-4: -The United Nations Conference on the Human Environment was held in Stockholm, Sweden, from June 5–16 in 1972.

Concept note-5: -First Earth summit which is also called as United Nations conference on environment and development was held in the year 1992 on 14th June.
