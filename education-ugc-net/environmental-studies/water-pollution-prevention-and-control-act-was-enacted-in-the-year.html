Water Pollution, ’Prevention and Control Act’ was enacted in the year

(A) 1954

(B) 1964

(C) ** 1974

(D) 1984

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act was enacted in 1974 to provide for the prevention and control of water pollution, and for the maintaining or restoring of wholesomeness of water in the country. The Act was amended in 1988.

Concept note-2: -Under Water Act 1974, Sewage or pollutants cannot be discharged into water bodies including lakes and it is the duty of the state pollution control board to intervene and stop such activity. The Act prohibits disposal of any poisonous, noxious or polluting matter to the flow of water in a stream.
