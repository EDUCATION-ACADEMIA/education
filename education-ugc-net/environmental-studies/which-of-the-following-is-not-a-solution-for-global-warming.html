Which of the following is not a solution for Global warming?

(A) ** Deforestation

(B) Plantation of more trees

(C) Reducing fossil fuel consumption

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The answer to the question is iii) deforestation. The other two options mentioned here can contribute to the solution of global warming.

Concept note-2: -For example, improvements to energy efficiency and vehicle fuel economy, increases in wind and solar power, biofuels from organic waste, setting a price on carbon, and protecting forests are all potent ways to reduce the amount of carbon dioxide and other gases trapping heat on the planet.

Concept note-3: -When forests are cut down, much of that stored carbon is released into the atmosphere again as carbon dioxide (CO2). This is how deforestation and forest degradation contribute to global warming.

Concept note-4: -But several lines of evidence show that current global warming cannot be explained by changes in energy from the Sun: Since 1750, the average amount of energy from the Sun either remained constant or increased slightly.

Concept note-5: -Change a light. Replacing one regular light bulb with a compact fluorescent light bulb will save 150 pounds of carbon dioxide a year. Drive less. Recycle more. Check your tires. Use less hot water. Avoid products with a lot of packaging. Adjust your thermostat. Plant a tree. More items
