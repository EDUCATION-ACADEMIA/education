Liquid waste generated from Bathrooms and Kitchens are called

(A) Runoff

(B) Sullage

(C) ** Domestic sewage

(D) All the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The wastewater from kitchens and bathrooms is called blackwater.

Concept note-2: -Domestic sewage is wastewater that is removed from the daily life of households, hotels, restaurants, schools, and shopping malls. It has a wide range of sources and a huge amount.

Concept note-3: -Explanation: Sullage: The water from the bathroom, kitchens washbasin and washing paces are known as sullage.

Concept note-4: -Sewage is mainly liquid waste. It is the wastewater containing both liquid and solid wastes produced by human activities from homes, industries, hospitals, offices, etc. It majorly consists of water, which consists of dissolved and suspended impurities called contaminants.

Concept note-5: -The term sewage is used to describe a combination of all these types of liquid waste, frequently also with surface run-off.
