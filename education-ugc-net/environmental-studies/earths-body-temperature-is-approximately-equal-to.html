Earth’s body temperature is approximately equal to

(A) ** 16.4 ∘ C

(B) 26.4 ∘ C

(C) 36.4 ∘ C

(D) 46.4 ∘ C

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer. Answer: The average surface temperature on Earth is approximately 14°C; but as already noted, this varies. For instance, the hottest temperature ever recorded on Earth was 70.7°C (159°F), which was taken in the Lut Desert of Iran.

Concept note-2: -What Is Normal Body Temperature? As per the study done in 1800, the normal body temperature of most people is 98.6 degrees Fahrenheit or 37 degrees Celsius. The average temperature of the body is noted somewhere between 97.5 F or 36.4 C and 97.9 F or 36.6 C.

Concept note-3: -Homeothermy is one of the three types of thermoregulation in warm-blooded animal species. Homeothermy’s opposite is poikilothermy. A poikilotherm is an organism that does not maintain a fixed internal temperature but rather fluctuates based on their environment and physical behaviour.
