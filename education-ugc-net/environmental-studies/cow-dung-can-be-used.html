Cow dung can be used

(A) as manure

(B) for production of biogas

(C) ** Both (A) and (B)

(D) none of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Dung is also used as fuel in the form of dried cakes for cooking and heating as well as for production of bio-gas. Slurry, which is a by-product of bio-gas is an eminently rich source of manure and is, in turn, usable for enrichment of the soil. Cow urine is also of immense medicinal value.

Concept note-2: -Complete answer: It is utilized as compost or pattings for fuel. The cow dung has antifungal and bug spray properties. The use of cow dung likewise improves soil saltiness and alkalinity.
