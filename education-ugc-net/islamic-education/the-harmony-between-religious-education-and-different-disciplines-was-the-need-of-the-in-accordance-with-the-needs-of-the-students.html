EDUCATION UGC NET

ISLAMIC EDUCATION

[SOURCES]
The harmony between religious education and different disciplines was the need of the ____in accordance with the needs of the students.

(A) Science

(B) Nation

(C) ** Time

(D) Educational System

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Education is central to religion. To be able to send, receive, and interpret the religious message one needs to be educated. Education is also central to the secular nation-state. The modern nation-state demands its subjects to be disciplined and educated in a national curriculum.

Concept note-2: -Religious harmony can be understood as friendly coexistence among different religions.

Concept note-3: -Discipline is a way to set limits for the children so that they know what is right and what is wrong. This improves the character of the students and they become strong as well as self-reliant. Discipline even develops the analytical skills of the students.

Concept note-4: -Religious and moral education enables children and young people to explore the world’s major religions and views which are independent of religious belief and to consider the challenges posed by these beliefs and values.
