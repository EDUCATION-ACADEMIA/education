Knowledge of ____ comes when a person gets awareness of religion through understanding recitation,and sanctification and becomes able to decide according to religion to get his God,s approval.

(A) ** Wisdom

(B) Recitation

(C) Islamic Culture

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What is religious knowledge? “Religious knowledge systems offer answers to fundamental questions about the meaning and purpose of human life. This area of knowledge incorporates a diverse range of different beliefs and systems; for example, varieties of theism, pantheism and polytheism.

Concept note-2: -Theology is defined by Merriam-Webster dictionary as being the study of religious faith, practice, and experience.

Concept note-3: -Sociologists are not concerned with whether religious beliefs are true or false. Religions are regarded as being socially constructed by human beings. They are concerned with the human rather than divine aspects of religion.

Concept note-4: -Methodological Atheism. The fundamental character of any social scientific approach to the study of religion that suspends belief in the divine.
