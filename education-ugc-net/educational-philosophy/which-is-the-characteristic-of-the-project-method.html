Which is the characteristic of the project method?

(A) Problematic act

(B) A voluntary undertaking

(C) Carried in its natural setting

(D) ** Used for all-round-development of child’s personality

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It involves investigating and solutions to problems frequently by the use and manipulation of physical material, planned and carried to completion by pupils and teachers in a natural real-life manner. It leads to an all-around development of the child’s personality.

Concept note-2: -The project method is a method of educating the child and, therefore, it must also be real. The desire for an activity must be spontaneous and not forced by the teacher. The child should be free from imposition, restrictions or obstruction so that he may express himself fully and freely.

Concept note-3: -Characteristics of Project Method: Relates learning with real-life. Promotes learner’s active participation. Develops abstract and concrete scientific skills.

Concept note-4: -Thus, from the above-mentioned points, it is clear that Objective interpretation is not a characteristic of a projective test.

Concept note-5: -A project has a definite attainable goal. It involves life-like and purposeful activities which promote learning. Students can plan and perform their learning activities. It promotes learning by doing. It is a play-way activity which enhances learning. More items •14-Nov-2017
