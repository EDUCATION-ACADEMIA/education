The material and the efficient cause, accor-ding to Samkhya philosophy, are related as

(A) Identical

(B) ** Different

(C) Contradictory

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Samkhya is the oldest of the orthodox philosophical schools, and it holds that everything, in reality, is derived from Purusha (self, soul, or intellect) and Prakriti (matter, creative agency, energy).

Concept note-2: -Sankhya philosophy teaches us that the universe was born from the union of Prakriti and Purusha. Prakriti here refers to the basic cosmic material that is the root of all beings, and Purusha to the spirit or conscious energy that governs life and reality.

Concept note-3: -Samkhya regards ignorance (avidyā) as the root cause of suffering and bondage (Samsara).

Concept note-4: -Satkaryavada is the Samkhya theory of the pre-existent effect, that the effect (karya) already exists in its material cause and therefore, nothing new is brought into existence or produced in the process of creation.
