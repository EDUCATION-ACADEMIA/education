Who emphasised realization of Truth, Beauty and Goodness as the aims of education?

(A) Realists

(B) ** Idealists

(C) Naturalists

(D) Pragmatists

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -"TRUTH, BEAUTY AND GOODNESS” IN THE PHILOSOPHY OF A. N. John Goheen, Whitehead’s Theory of Value, The Philosophy of Alfred North Whitehead (P. A. Schilpp Ed.), pp.

Concept note-2: -Rabindranath Tagore believed that the aim of education is self-realization. He himself was a poet and a saint, who had, through his imagination and insight, realized the universal soul in himself and in nature.

Concept note-3: -According to idealism, man is the most beautiful creature of God his grandest work. It lays stress on the exaltation of human personality. It is self-realization. The aim of education is to develop the self of the individual’s higher till self-realization is achieved.
