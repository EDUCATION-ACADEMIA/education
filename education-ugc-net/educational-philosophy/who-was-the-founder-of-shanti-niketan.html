Who was the founder of Shanti Niketan?

(A) Gandhi

(B) ** Tagore

(C) Sri Aurobindo

(D) Vivekananda

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On 22nd December 1901, Rabindranath Tagore established his school at Santiniketan with five students (including his eldest son) and an equal number of teachers. He originally named it Brahmacharya Ashram in the tradition of ancient forest hermitages.

Concept note-2: -Shantiniketan began as a meditation centre called Shantiniketan Ashram. It was founded by Debendranath Tagore, the father of Rabindranath Tagore and son of Dwarkanath Tagore.

Concept note-3: -Shantiniketan was founded and developed by members of the Tagore family. It was founded by Debendranath Tagore. Rabindranath Tagore wrote many of his literary classics at Santiniketan. His son, Rathindranath Tagore was one of the first five students at the Brahmacharya asrama at Santiniketan.

Concept note-4: -Shantiniketan began as a meditation centre called Shantiniketan Ashram. It was founded by Debendranath Tagore, the father of Rabindranath Tagore and son of Dwarkanath Tagore.
