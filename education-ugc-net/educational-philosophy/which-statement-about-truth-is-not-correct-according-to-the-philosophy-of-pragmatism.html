Which statement about truth is not correct according to the philosophy of Pragmatism?

(A) ** It is eternal

(B) It is made by man

(C) It is ever changing

(D) It is what emerges to be true in actual practice

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hence, Pragmatism accepts any definite/eternal truth or principle is not correct regarding pragmatism.

Concept note-2: -A Pragmatic Theory of Truth holds (roughly) that a proposition is true if it is useful to believe. Peirce and James were its principal advocates. Utility is the essential mark of truth.

Concept note-3: -In education, pragmatism is an approach to learning and teaching that focuses on keeping things practical. Its key theorist is John Dewey. It has four principles: Unity, Interest, Experience, and Integration.

Concept note-4: -A pragmatist can consider something to be true without needing to confirm that it is universally true. For example, if humans commonly perceive the ocean as beautiful then the ocean is beautiful.
