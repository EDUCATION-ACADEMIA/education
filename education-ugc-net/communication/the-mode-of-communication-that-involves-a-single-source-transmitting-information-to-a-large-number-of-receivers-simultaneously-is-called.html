COMMUNICATION

[SOURCES]
The mode of communication that involves a single source transmitting information to a large number of receivers simultaneously, is called

(A) ** Mass Communication

(B) Group Communication

(C) Interpersonal Communication

(D) Intrapersonal Communication

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Mass communication. It is also known as Public communication. In other words, it is a process of transferring information to large audiences using verbal and written media.

Concept note-2: -(c)Intrapersonal Communication.

Concept note-3: -Mass communication is the process of imparting and exchanging information through mass media to large population segments. It is understood as relating to various forms of media, as its technologies are used for the dissemination of information, of which journalism and advertising are part.

Concept note-4: -In public communication, Source or messages from a single person will reach or received by huge number of audience. But in this communication there is no mutual feedbacks between source and receiver like small group communication and itâ€™s only focused on Speaker.

Concept note-5: -Massive Audiences: One of the characteristics of mass communication, which makes it different from others is it deals with massive audiences. It conveys a message to thousands or billions of people. Anonymous Audiences: A message gets conveyed to a massive audience, which is unknown to the communicator.
