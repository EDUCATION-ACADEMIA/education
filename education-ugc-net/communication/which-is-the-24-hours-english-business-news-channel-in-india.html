Which is the 24 hours English Business news channel in India?

(A) ** CNBC

(B) Zee News

(C) India News

(D) NDTV 24 × 7

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NDTV 24x7 is a 24-hour English-language television news channel based in New Delhi, India.

Concept note-2: -CNBC World is a 24 hour digital television network offering live, global financial market information and programming.

Concept note-3: -Budget. Watch ET Now News (ETNow) Budget Business Financial News Channel Live.ET Now is the India’s No.1 Business News Channel, Owned & Operated By The Times Network.
