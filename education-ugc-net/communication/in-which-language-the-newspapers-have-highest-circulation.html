In which language the newspapers have highest circulation?

(A) Urdu

(B) ** Hindi

(C) Bengali

(D) English

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: –Newspapers in the Hindi language are by far the most widely circulated, but there are publications produced in each of India’s 22 scheduled languages and in many of the other languages spoken throughout the country.

Concept note-2: -The Times of India is the most popular English newspaper in India, with a circulation of over 45 lakh copies. Dainik Jagran is India’s most popular Hindi newspaper, with over 35 lakh daily readers across the country.

Concept note-3: -Udant Martand (from Hindi, “The Rising Sun”) is the first Hindi language newspaper published in India. Started on 30 May 1826, from Calcutta (now Kolkata), the weekly newspaper was published every Tuesday by Pt. Jugal Kishore Shukla.

Concept note-4: -There are more than 100000 registered publications and newspapers in India.
