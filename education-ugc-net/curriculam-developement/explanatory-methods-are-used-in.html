Explanatory methods are used in?

(A) ** Subject centered curriculum

(B) Learner centered curriculum

(C) Activity centered curriculum

(D) None

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The subject-centered approach is one of the most widely used methods for organizing educational experiences. In this approach, the subject matter becomes the basis around which learning experiences are organized and the mastery of subject matter becomes the basis for the attainment of educational objectives.

Concept note-2: -Subject-centered curriculum design refers to reviewing the skills, ideas and facts that are critical to a given subject area. Learning activities are developed around these facts.

Concept note-3: -Subject-centered designs include subject designs, discipline designs, broad field designs and process designs.

Concept note-4: -Subject-centered curriculum design revolves around a particular subject matter or discipline, such as mathematics, literature or biology. This model of curriculum design tends to focus on the subject, rather than the student.
