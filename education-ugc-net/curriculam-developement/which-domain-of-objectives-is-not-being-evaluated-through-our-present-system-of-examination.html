Which domain of objectives is not being evaluated through our present system of examination:

(A) ** Affective

(B) Cognitive

(C) Psychomotor

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Affective domain-In the past system of education there is no such scope to assess a child over this affective domain.

Concept note-2: -Thus, it is concluded that the Present Examination system mainly measures the objectives of the cognitive domain.

Concept note-3: -Hence, it could be concluded that the is knowledge is not related to the affective domain of Bloom’s taxonomy of educational objectives. NOTE: Knowledge is related to the cognitive domain of Bloom’s taxonomy of educational objectives.

Concept note-4: -Hence, it could be concluded that the is Analysis is not included in the affective aspect. Analysis is related to the cognitive aspect/domain of Bloom’s taxonomy of educational objectives.
