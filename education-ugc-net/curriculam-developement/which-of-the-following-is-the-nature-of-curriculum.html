Which of the following is the nature of curriculum?

(A) Critical

(B) Creative

(C) Conservative

(D) ** All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Expert-Verified Answer. The nature of curriculum is d) all the above. Explanation: The proper and intentional organisation of the building blocks required for a class to run effectively is referred to as curriculum design.

Concept note-2: -By Definition. It is as dynamic as change. It is a listing of subjects to be taught in school. It is a total learning experiences of a person in and out of the school setting.

Concept note-3: -A curriculum is the combination of instructional practices, learning experiences, and students’ performance assessments that are designed to bring out and evaluate the target learning outcomes of a particular course.

Concept note-4: -"Curriculum theory” is a term for how an educational institution decides what is at worth to learn and teach, and how learning will be measured. Pedagogy is closely related to curriculum theory, but where pedagogy describes how people teach and learn, curriculum describes how people decide what to teach and learn.

Concept note-5: -Subject-centered Curriculum Design. Learner-centered Curriculum Design. Problem-centered Curriculum Design. 06-Jun-2019
