If a child has mental age of 5 years and chronological age of 4 years than what will be the IQ of child?

(A) ** 125

(B) 80

(C) 100

(D) 120

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hence, we conclude that if a student’s chronological age is 4 years and the mental age is 5 years, his I.Q. will be 125.

Concept note-2: -mental age, intelligence test score, expressed as the chronological age for which a given level of performance is average or typical. An individual’s mental age is then divided by his chronological age and multiplied by 100, yielding an intelligence quotient (IQ).

Concept note-3: -The IQ of person is given by the formula, IQ=mc×100, where m is the mental age and c is the chromological age. If 80≤IQ≤140 for a group of 12-year children, find the range of their mental age.

Concept note-4: -Thus, a subject whose mental and chronological ages are identical has an IQ of 100, or average intelligence.
