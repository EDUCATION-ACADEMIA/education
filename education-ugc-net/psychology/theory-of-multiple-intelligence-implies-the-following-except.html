Theory of multiple intelligence implies the following except

(A) intelligence is a distinct set of processing operations used by an individual to solve problems.

(B) disciplines should be presented in a number of ways

(C) learning could be assessed through a variety of means

(D) ** emotional intelligence is not related to IQ

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hence it is clear that the theory of multiple intelligence implies above all given options except emotional intelligence as it is not related to IQ. The Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) announced the CTET Result on 3rd March 2023.

Concept note-2: -Howard Gardner’s theory of multiple intelligences proposes that people are not born with all of the intelligence they will ever have. This theory challenged the traditional notion that there is one single type of intelligence, sometimes known as “g” for general intelligence, that only focuses on cognitive abilities.

Concept note-3: -What Is Emotional Intelligence? Emotional intelligence describes a person’s capacity to recognize and contextualize their emotions and the emotions of others. The concept stems from Dr. Howard Gardner’s Theory of Multiple Intelligences.

Concept note-4: -Thus, we consider that emotional intelligence is not included in the eight types of intelligence proposed by Howard Gardner.

Concept note-5: -Hence, we conclude that the Theory of regression is not the theory of intelligence. It is a part of statistical analysis.
