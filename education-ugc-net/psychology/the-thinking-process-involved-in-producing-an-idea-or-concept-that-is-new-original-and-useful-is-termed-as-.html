The thinking process involved in producing an idea or concept that is new, original and useful is termed as-

(A) ** Creativity

(B) Innovation

(C) Intelligence

(D) Synectics

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hence, it could be concluded that the thinking process involved in producing an idea or concept that is new, original and useful is termed as Creativity. Innovation is the spark of insight that leads a scientist or inventor to investigate an issue or phenomenon.

Concept note-2: -Thinking, also known as ‘cognition’, refers to the ability to process information, hold attention, store and retrieve memories and select appropriate responses and actions. The ability to understand other people, and express oneself to others can also be categorised under thinking.

Concept note-3: -Innovation is the practical implementation of ideas that result in the introduction of new goods or services or improvement in offering goods or services. ISO TC 279 in the standard ISO 56000:2020 defines innovation as “a new or changed entity realizing or redistributing value".

Concept note-4: -Divergent and convergent thinking are the most common ways to foster more creative thought. Divergent thinking is like a traditional brainstorming session, where you come up with as many possible solutions as your imagination will allow.

Concept note-5: -Creative thinking is the ability to consider something in a new way. Creative thinking includes analysis, open-mindedness, problem-solving, organization, and communication. Many employers value creative thinkers, so consider highlighting your creative thinking skills on your resume and in interviews.
