Discipline means:

(A) strict-behaviour

(B) severe punishment

(C) obedience

(D) ** going by the rules

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -: to punish or penalize for the sake of enforcing obedience and perfecting moral character. : to train or develop by instruction and exercise especially in self-control.

Concept note-2: -Discipline is one of the most important personality traits in everyone’s life. It refers to a set of rules and regulations which are to be followed while undergoing any task or activity. It is a way of being honest, hard-working, motivated, and encouraged while doing any task.

Concept note-3: -The Three Types: Preventive Discipline–measures taken to preempt misbehavior by keeping students engaged. Supportive Discipline–measures taken to assist students with self-control by helping them get back on task. Corrective Discipline–measures taken when students are not following classroom or school rules.

Concept note-4: -Discipline in student life means working with all honesty with strict adherence to rules and regulations, cultural standards, and values. Many other attributes and character traits come from discipline.
