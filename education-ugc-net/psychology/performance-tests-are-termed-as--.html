Performance tests are termed as ____ .

(A) Verbal tests

(B) ** Non-verbal tests

(C) Projective tests

(D) Subjective tests

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Performance test, also known as a non-verbal intelligence test is a test that: assesses learner’s ability to understand and interpret visual information like pictures or diagrams.

Concept note-2: -Performance testing is a testing measure that evaluates the speed, responsiveness and stability of a computer, network, software program or device under a workload. Organizations will run performance tests to identify performance-related bottlenecks.

Concept note-3: -A verbal (or symbol) test poses questions to which the subject supplies symbolic answers (in words or in other symbols, such as numbers). In performance tests, the subject actually executes some motor activity; for example, he assembles mechanical objects.

Concept note-4: -Performance Intelligence Quotient (PIQ) is a score resulting from a test that assesses your child’s mental capacity in dealing with nonverbal skills.
