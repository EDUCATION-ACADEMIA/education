Some students send a greeting card to you on teacher’s day. What will you do? You will-

(A) do nothing

(B) ** say thanks to them

(C) ask them to not to waste money

(D) reciprocate the good wishes to them

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -If students gift greeting cards to the teacher on the occasion of Teacher’s Day, the teacher must thank them. Rajasthan Pre D. El. Ed result declared.

Concept note-2: -I feel so uniquely fortunate that you’re my student. I appreciate you so much for sending me some wishes on teacher’s day. I am especially privileged to instruct students such as you. I am very grateful for your warm wishes.

Concept note-3: -Thank you very much, indeed. It was clever of you to pick out something so perfect for me. I enjoyed having you in my class this year and appreciated all the effort you put into your work. I am sure you will go on to do great things since you are talented and smart, too.

Concept note-4: -Thank you of course. You should thank them for the greetings because they remembered you (if you are a teacher).

Concept note-5: -“We appreciate all the work you’ve put into teaching our child this year. “Thanks for being the best teacher ever. “Thank you for making my first year of school so special! “Thank you for being such a wonderful teacher to our child this year! More items
