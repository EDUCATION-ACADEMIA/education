Concept note-1: -Co-education refers to the integrated education of both boys and girls together in the same institution. Some benefits of co-education: Boys and girls are not separated rather they are educated together. No special preference is given to any particular gender as it is an all-inclusive way of growing together.

Concept note-2: -The co-education system is a necessity in the present times due to several reasons. Under this system, boys and girls receive physical, moral values and academic education together. They learn to respect each other and interact with the opposite sex without any feeling of superiority.

Concept note-3: -Coeducation schooling, also called mixed-sex schooling, is the practice of educating males and females together. Single-sex schooling, on the other hand, refers to a form of education in which the two sexes are separated when teaching is conducted.
