Which of the following qualities of a teacher will be liked most by you?

(A) ** Compassion

(B) Entertaining

(C) Idealistic philosophy

(D) Discipline

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Thus, compassionate teachers, are most liked by the students.

Concept note-2: -Hence, a loving teacher will be liked most by her students because a disciplinarian teacher or one with a high idealistic attitude is likely to unsettle the average students. A teacher who amuses in class risks being taken lightly by the overenthusiastic and audacious students.

Concept note-3: -A compassionate teacher teaches from the heart. The heart knows what it is like to have a passion for the subject. The compassionate kindly teaches, guiding, and allowing students to explore to discover errors and revelations. The compassionate teacher models the qualities of the great teachers of history.

Concept note-4: -Other qualities of a teacher: Devoted and dedicated. Responsible and caring. Impartial to all children.

Concept note-5: -Patient and approachable. They say that ‘patience is a virtue’, and this couldn’t be more true for teachers. Enthusiastic. Strong communication skills. Strong knowledge and a solid education. Disciplined and professional. 01-Mar-2022
