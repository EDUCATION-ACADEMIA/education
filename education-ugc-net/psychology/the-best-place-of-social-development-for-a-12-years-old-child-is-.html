The best place of social development for a 12 years old child is ____

(A) Neighborhood

(B) Family

(C) ** Playground

(D) School

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Twelve-year-olds are starting to spend their free time on activities such as organized sports, video games, and social activities with friends. So, the playground is the best place for the social development of preteens which helps to be socialized, learn teamwork, and build up communication skills too.

Concept note-2: -Social development in infancy: The family is a key system within the larger social system. The first social relationship most infants form is with a parent and in most cultures that parent is the mother.

Concept note-3: -Developing social skills through play Playing can help children develop their social skills with others. By listening, paying attention and sharing play experiences, this helps a child: explore their feelings. develop self-discipline.

Concept note-4: -Social play begins early with smiling and cooing, imitation and peek a boo. Children then learn to share toys during play with others and they continue to develop social skills that involve turn taking and following the rules.

Concept note-5: -Share toys and take turns. Begin engaging in pretend play. Follow simple rules in games. Sometimes become bossy and defiant. Show more independence. Might show attachment to one friend. 03-May-2017
