When some students are deliberately attempting to disturb the discipline of the class by making mischief, what will be your role as a teacher?

(A) Expelling those students.

(B) Isolate those students.

(C) Reform the group with your authority.

(D) ** Giving them an opportunity for introspection and improve their behaviour.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -When some students are deliberately attempting to disturb the discipline of the class by making mischief, what will be your role as a teacher? Expelling those students.

Concept note-2: -Be Proactive With Discipline Problems Give the students a few moments to work things out before you start your lesson. Separate them if necessary and try to gain agreement that during your class period at least, they will drop the issue. Post a discipline plan that you follow consistently to govern student conduct.

Concept note-3: -Give and get trust. Some times giving a chance for the irregular students to be regular is giving them your trust than inducing fear. Hope for best, prepare for worst. Give them care than hatred. Spread positivity. Be their role model. 27-Jul-2017

Concept note-4: -Create Consistency. Students of all ages will react positively to a consistent approach to discipline. Make Sure Punishments and Rewards Are Clear. Don’t Reward Disruption With Attention. Keep Things Exciting. Wipe The Slate Clean. 07-Dec-2020

Concept note-5: -1) Clear rules about what is acceptable and what is not. 2) Rapport. 3) Learning students’ names is highly important. 4) Develop a flexible teaching style, and recognise when students aren’t interested in the lesson you have prepared for them! 5) Build a team atmosphere.
