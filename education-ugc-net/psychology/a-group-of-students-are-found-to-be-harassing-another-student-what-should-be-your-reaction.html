A group of students are found to be harassing another student. What should be your reaction?

(A) ** Punish the students

(B) Send to their parents

(C) Counsel the students individually

(D) Give them a severe warning

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Try to inculcate the skill of empathy in students. Find out what is causing such disruptive behaviour by speaking to their parents. Start open conversations regarding bullying and harassment. Provide positive reinforcement when such students display good behaviour.

Concept note-2: -The best way to handle a wrong response from a student would be to explain and give another chance to give the answer. This strategy will help the student to listen and understand the concept again and will also help the teacher to assess the other learnerâ€™s understanding of the concept.

Concept note-3: -Cool off. First, before problem-solving can begin, the students need time to calm down. Share, listen, check. Students need to listen to each other share their issues, and then check that they understand them. Take responsibility. Brainstorm solutions. Choose a solution. Affirm, forgive, or thank. 11-Nov-2020

Concept note-4: -Move on. Pull aside. Avoid confrontation. Recount and inform. Model the alternative. Pause. Make a promise. 08-Nov-2014

Concept note-5: -Bring difficult students close to you. Talk to them in private. Be the role model of the behavior you want. Define right from wrong. Focus more on rewards than punishments. Adopt the peer tutor technique. Try to understand.
