____ is a preferred sampling method for the population with finite size.

(A) ** Systematic sampling

(B) Purposive sampling

(C) Cluster sampling

(D) Area sampling

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -7. is a preferred sampling method for the population with finite size. Random selection is done in systematic sampling.

Concept note-2: -quota sampling When the sampling requires selecting a fixed size of the population, this type of sampling is used.

Concept note-3: -Solution(By Examveda Team) Systematic sampling technique is generally followed when the population is finite.

Concept note-4: -We could choose a sampling method based on whether we want to account for sampling bias; a random sampling method is often preferred over a non-random method for this reason. Random sampling examples include: simple, systematic, stratified, and cluster sampling.

Concept note-5: -Systematic sampling is a probability sampling method where researchers select members of the population at a regular interval – for example, by selecting every 15th person on a list of the population. If the population is in a random order, this can imitate the benefits of simple random sampling.
