RESEARCH METHODOLOGY

[SOURCES]
Which of the following statements regarding the meaning of research are correct

(A) It leads to enhancement of knowledge

(B) Research refers to a series of systematic activity or activities undertaken to find out the solution of a problem

(C) It is a systematic, logical and an unbiased process wherein verification of hypothesis data analysis, interpretation and formation of principles can be done

(D) ** All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Research refers to a series of systematic activity, or activities undertaken to find out the solution to a problem. II. It is a systematic, logical and unbiased process wherein verification of hypothesis, data analysis, interpretation and formation of principle can be done.

Concept note-2: -Research is a process of systematic inquiry that entails collection of data; documentation of critical information; and analysis and interpretation of that data/information, in accordance with suitable methodologies set by specific professional fields and academic disciplines.

Concept note-3: -especially : investigation or experimentation aimed at the discovery and interpretation of facts, revision of accepted theories or laws in the light of new facts, or practical application of such new or revised theories or laws. : the collecting of information about a particular subject. 3. : careful or diligent search.

Concept note-4: -Thus, the correct statement that best reflects the meaning and characteristics of research are: (a) Research means challenging the status quo. (c) Research is a synthesis of deductive and inductive processes.
