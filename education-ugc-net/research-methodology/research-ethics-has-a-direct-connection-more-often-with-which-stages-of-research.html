RESEARCH METHODOLOGY

[SOURCES]
Research ethics has a direct connection more often with which stages of research?

(A) Defining and delimiting the scope of research.

(B) Deciding about statistical techniques and data analysis.

(C) ** Problem formulation and reporting of research findings.

(D) Defining the population and deciding the sampling technique for research.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Research ethics has a direct connection more often with which stages of research? Defining and delimiting the scope of research.

Concept note-2: -Conclusion: The issues of research ethics become relevant in Selecting a problem and Data analysis and interpretation.

Concept note-3: -Research ethics concerns with issues related to what is appropriate in the conduct of research. This involves consideration of how research should treat the people who form the subjects if their investigation.

Concept note-4: -Therefore, Data analysis, interpretation, and reporting of results may be considered as the most vulnerable stage in terms of research ethics.

Concept note-5: -Discuss intellectual property frankly. Be conscious of multiple roles. Follow informed-consent rules. Respect confidentiality and privacy. Tap into ethics resources.
