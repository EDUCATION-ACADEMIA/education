Which of the following methods will you choose to prepare choropleth map of India showing urban density of population:

(A) Quintiles

(B) ** Quartiles

(C) Break - point

(D) Mean and SD

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Complete Answer: The dot method is considered a better method to show distribution since it is accurate as compared to other methods. Dot maps can be converted to Choropleth and Isopleth maps but Chloropleth and Isopleth maps cannot be transferred into dot maps.

Concept note-2: -Answer 4: The distribution of population density in India can be represented by the choropleth map.

Concept note-3: -The density of population in a region is shown on choropleth map by shades, patterns or tints of multiple colors.

Concept note-4: -The choropleth method is a method where data regarding various geographical variables is represented by shades or tints of various colours.
