[SOURCES]
Which of the following statements is not true in the context of participatory research?

(A) It recognizes knowledge as power.

(B) It is a collective process of enquiry.

(C) It emphasises on people as experts.

(D) ** Its sole purpose is production of knowledge.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Explanation: Its sole purpose is production of knowledge.

Concept note-2: -Participatory research integrates scientific investigation with education and political action. Researchers work with members of a community to understand and resolve community problems, to empower community members, and to democratize research.

Concept note-3: -Context. Participatory research (PR) is the co-construction of research through partnerships between researchers and people affected by and/or responsible for action on the issues under study.
