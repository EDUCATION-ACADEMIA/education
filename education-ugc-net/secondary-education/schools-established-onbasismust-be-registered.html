Schools established on____basis,must be registered.

(A) ** Commercial

(B) Economical

(C) Mercantile

(D) Educational

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In India, educational institutions are mandatorily established by a Trust or Society or a Section 8 company.

Concept note-2: -According to laws, private associations can’t open Private schools in India. The schools must be worked by a general public that has been made as per the Societies Act of 1860 under society registration or by an assumption that has been sorted out according to the Public Trust Act of individual states.

Concept note-3: -The initial and most important process to establish an educational institution is formal registration. As per the Constitution of India, education institutions must be registered under the name of a Trust or Society only. Private institutions may create the institution as per Section 8 under the Companies Act, 2013.
