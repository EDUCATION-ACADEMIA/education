On how many weeks,an academic year must be consisting?

(A) 35

(B) 42

(C) ** 40

(D) 43

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The entire academic year lasts for around 30 weeks. A semester system is being implemented in most of the universities in India as directed by the University Grants Commission. In universities the academic year is from July to May.

Concept note-2: -Typically, an average U.S. school year has approximately 25 weeks or 180 days. This article covers school durations for numerous countries, popular holidays/days off, and classroom practices for teachers to implement throughout the 180-day school year.

Concept note-3: -England school year has 39 weeks or 195 days (5 days inset days) over 3 terms split into 6 half terms with school holidays between terms.

Concept note-4: -A general guideline is approximately 36 weeks of instruction a year, which comes out to about 180 school days a year.
