PSYCHOLOGICAL DISORDERS

[SOURCES]
In autistic spectrum disorder when as individual exhibits immediate imitation of words or sound they have just heard this is known as.

(A) Echoastic disorder

(B) Phonological inhibition

(C) ** Echolalia

(D) Grapheme dysfunction

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Many children on the autism spectrum use echolalia, which means they repeat others’ words or sentences. They might repeat the words of familiar people (parents, teachers), or they might repeat sentences from their favourite video. When children repeat words right after they hear them, it’s known as immediate echolalia.

Concept note-2: -29) In autistic spectrum disorder when as individual exhibits immediate imitation of words or sounds they have just heard, this is known as: a) Echoastic disorder.

Concept note-3: -Some types of developmental disorders include: ADHD. Autism spectrum disorder. Cerebral palsy.

Concept note-4: -The common types of intellectual disabilities include autism, Down syndrome, fragile x syndrome, fetal alcohol syndrome, and Prader-Willi syndrome.

Concept note-5: -A diagnosis of autistic disorder is made when there are impairments in communication and reciprocal social interaction with the presence of restricted repetitive and stereotyped patterns of behaviors or interests, prior to the age of 3 years.
