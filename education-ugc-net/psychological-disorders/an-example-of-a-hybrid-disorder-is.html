An example of a hybrid disorder is.

(A) ** Mixed anxiety-depressive disorder

(B) Schizoidal personality disorder

(C) Multiple personality disorder

(D) Borderline personality disorder

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Correct! Mixed Anxiety-Depressive Disorder: A hybrid disorder exhibiting symptoms of both anxiety and depression.

Concept note-2: -Mental illness, also called mental health disorders, refers to a wide range of mental health conditions-disorders that affect your mood, thinking and behavior. Examples of mental illness include depression, anxiety disorders, schizophrenia, eating disorders and addictive behaviors.

Concept note-3: -So, the correct answer is ‘Gout’.

Concept note-4: -Axis I disorders tend to be the most commonly found in the public. They include anxiety disorders, such as panic disorder, social anxiety disorder, and post-traumatic stress disorder. Other examples of Axis I disorders are as follows: Dissociative disorders.
