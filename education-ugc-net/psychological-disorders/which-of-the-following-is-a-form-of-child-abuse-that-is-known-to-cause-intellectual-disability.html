Which of the following is a form of child abuse that is known to cause intellectual disability?

(A) ** Shaken baby syndrome

(B) Abused child syndrome

(C) Battered baby syndrome

(D) Damaged infant syndrome

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It’s also known as abusive head trauma, shaken impact syndrome, inflicted head injury or whiplash shaken infant syndrome. Shaken baby syndrome destroys a child’s brain cells and prevents his or her brain from getting enough oxygen. This form of child abuse can cause permanent brain damage or death.

Concept note-2: -Some of the most common known causes of intellectual disability – like Down syndrome, fetal alcohol syndrome, fragile X syndrome, genetic conditions, birth defects, and infections – happen before birth. Others happen while a baby is being born or soon after birth.

Concept note-3: -The common types of intellectual disabilities include autism, Down syndrome, fragile x syndrome, fetal alcohol syndrome, and Prader-Willi syndrome.

Concept note-4: -The most common known preventable or environmental cause of intellectual disability is fetal alcohol syndrome, the most common chromosomal cause is Down syndrome, and the most common genetic cause is Fragile X syndrome.

Concept note-5: -Down syndrome, the most common cause of ID in the United States. Fragile X syndrome, the most commonly known congenital (present at birth) cause of ID. Dravet syndrome, a rare congenital cause of ID. Huntington’s disease, a hereditary cause of late-onset ID.
