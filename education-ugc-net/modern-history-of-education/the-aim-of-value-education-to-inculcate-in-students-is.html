The aim of value education to inculcate in students is

(A) ** the moral values

(B) the social values

(C) the political values

(D) the economic values

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The value education aims to inculcate in students’ Moral Values. Value Education is also known as Moral Science. It is the subject where we learn Moral values.

Concept note-2: -Hence, The explicit objective of value education is to inculcate moral values.

Concept note-3: -Value Education through Schools For example, National Movement can be taught in such a way that it leads to inculcate the values of patriotism, secularism, universal love and tolerance etc.

Concept note-4: -Moral values are defined as guidelines that assist a person in deciding between right and wrong. In order to create honest, credible, and fair judgments and relationships in daily life, the awareness of one’s morals-along with self-awareness-is crucial.

Concept note-5: -Moral education helps one to distinguish between right and wrong. It gets reflected in one’s personality. It helps in building a good personal and professional life. It helps to eliminate problems like violence, dishonesty, jealousy etc from one’s life.
