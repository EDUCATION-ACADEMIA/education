The first Open University in India was set up in the State of

(A) ** Andhra Pradesh

(B) Delhi

(C) Himachal Pradesh

(D) Tamil Nadu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Established in 1982, the Andhra Pradesh Open University (APOU), now called Dr. B. R. Ambedkar Open University (Dr. BRAOU), was the first full-fledged open university to be set up in India.

Concept note-2: -Dr B.R Ambedkar Open University in Hyderabad, Andhra Pradesh set up on 26th August 1982, is the First Open University in India.

Concept note-3: -Allahabad, U.P. Hence, The first State Open University established in 1982 India is Bhim Rao Ambedkar Open University, Hyderabad. UGC NET Admit Card (Dec 2022 Phase 1) Out on 17th Feb 2023!
