EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -THE CENTRAL ADVISORY BOARD OF EDUCATION (CABE) The Central Advisory Board of Education, the oldest and the most important advisory body of the Government of India in education was first established in 1920 and dissolved in 1923 as a measure of economy. It was revived in 1935 and has been in existence ever since.

Concept note-2: -The CABE was established in 1920 and is the oldest advisory body on education in India. Its mandate includes providing advice on curriculum development, teacher education, and other matters related to the improvement of education in the country.
