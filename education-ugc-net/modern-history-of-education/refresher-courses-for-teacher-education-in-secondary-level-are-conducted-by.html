Refresher courses for teacher education in secondary level are conducted by

(A) National Council for Teacher Education

(B) National Institute of Education

(C) Academic Staff Colleges

(D) ** State Institutes of Education

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Refresher course & orientation programe are key programe to help continuous professional development of in-service teacher education and maintain quality of teaching profession. This study also help to understand the role of RC & OP to continuous professional development of in-service teacher.

Concept note-2: -In India, the accreditation of teacher education institutions and programs is managed by two institutions, the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) and the National Council on Teacher Education (NCTE).

Concept note-3: -The orientation programme will be of Three weeks duration, with minimum of 18 working days and 108 contact hours (six hours a day, six days a week). The refresher course will be of Two weeks duration, with minimum of 12 working days and 72 contact hours (six hours a day, six days a week), excluding Sundays.

Concept note-4: -A refresher training course is designed to take learners back to basics so they can review fundamentals they may have forgotten or upskill on new information they may be unaware of.
