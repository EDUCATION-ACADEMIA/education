One of the following organisations was not associated with National Education Movement in the early twentieth century.

(A) Bhagabat Chatuspadi of Satischandra Mukherjee

(B) The Saraswat Ayatan of Brahma Bandopadhyay

(C) Santiniketan Ashram of Rabindranath Tagore

(D) ** Anushilan Samiti of Barindra Kumar Ghosh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is option 3, i.e Lala Lajpat Rai . Only Lala Lajpat Rai is not associated with ‘Anushilan Samiti’ among given options. Anushilan Samiti was an Indian organisation that supported revolutionary violence as the means for ending British rule in India.

Concept note-2: -The policy provides for reforms at all levels of education from school to higher education. NEP aims to increase the focus on strengthening teacher training, reforming the existing exam system, early childhood care and restructuring the regulatory framework of education.

Concept note-3: -This sweeping reform of India’s education system was announced by the Indian government in July 2020. It replaces the previous National Policy on Education, which was formulated in 1986 and modified in 1992.

Concept note-4: -The policy called for fulfilling compulsory education for all children up to the age of 14, as stipulated by the Constitution of India and specialized training and qualification of teachers.
