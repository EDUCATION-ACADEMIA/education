Which of the following universities has NOT adopted the meta university concept?

(A) Assam University

(B) ** Delhi University

(C) Hyderabad University

(D) Pondicherry University

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Delhi University – The first institution to join the meta university movement.

Concept note-2: -Delhi University will first test the ‘meta college’ idea. The idea is to allow students and teachers to access and share teaching material, scholarly publications, research work, scientific work, virtual experiments etc, says the concept note.

Concept note-3: -What Is a Meta-University? As conceived by Charles Vest (2007), the meta–university would function as a distributed and decentralized network of universities.
