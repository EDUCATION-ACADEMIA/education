M.S. University -

(A) Sagar

(B) ** Baroda

(C) Mumbai

(D) Jodhpur

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The University was established by the Government of Tamil Nadu as a teaching-cum-affiliating University on 7th September, 1990 to cater to the long-felt needs of the people of the three southern most districts of Tamil Nadu viz., Tirunelveli, Tenkasi, Thoothukudi, and Kanyakumari.

Concept note-2: -Commonly know as MS university is one of the best university non only of Vadodara but of whole India. Most of faculties are available including Technology, Medical, Pharma, Commerce, Arts, Law, Mangment, Desigining not lost but least Faculty of fine Arts.

Concept note-3: -For securing admission into the MSU Baroda, candidates need to appear for the CMAT. After getting a valid score in the CMAT, Candidates can visit ACPC for the counselling process. At least a score of 160 is required to secure a seat at MSU Baroda for MBA.

Concept note-4: -MSU is the only state University with a residential, unitary character having English as sole medium of instruction with more than 1200 well-qualified faculty members and 1500 administrative staff to facilitate the learning of more than 35000 students having cosmopolitan life on campus.
