Which of the following institutions are empowered to confer or grant degrees under the UGC Act, 1956?

(A) A university established by an Act of Legislature.

(B) A university established by an Act of Parliament.

(C) An institution which is a deemed to be university.

(D) ** All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hence, from the given points, it is clear that the institutions established under linguistic minority, Constituent Colleges and Affiliated Colleges are not authorized to confer degrees according to the UGC Act 1956.

Concept note-2: -Universities are established by Central/State Act under Section 2(f) of the UGC Act, 1956 whereas an Institution Deemed to be University is declared by a Notification of the Central Government, on the advice of UGC, under Section 3 of the UGC Act, 1956.

Concept note-3: -Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani. Vidyasagar University. University of Delhi. APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University. Anna University. University of Mumbai. Karnataka State Law University. Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada. More items
