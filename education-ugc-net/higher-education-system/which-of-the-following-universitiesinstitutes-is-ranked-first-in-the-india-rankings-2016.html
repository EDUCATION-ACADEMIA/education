Which of the following universities/institutes is ranked first in the India Rankings 2016?

(A) Central University, Hyderabad

(B) ** Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore

(C) Jawaharlal Nehru University, New Delhi

(D) Institute of Chemical Technology, Mumbai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -IISc, Bangalore is the top-ranked university in India and has grabbed the first rank in NIRF ranking 2022 .

Concept note-2: -Rankings & ratings Indian Institute of Science is one of the top public universities in Bangalore, India. It is ranked #=155 in QS World University Rankings 2023.

Concept note-3: -IISc Bangalore also topped in common overall ranking in NIRF 2017.

Concept note-4: -Among the leading educational institutions across India in 2022, Indian Institute of Technology or IIT for short, in the southern city of Chennai had the highest ranking, followed by Indian Institute of Science in Bangalore.
