Information, a combination of graphics, text, sound, video and animation is called:

(A) Multifacet

(B) ** Multimedia

(C) Multiprocess

(D) Multiprogramme

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Multimedia is the name given to those applications that combine text, sound, graphics, motion video and animation.

Concept note-2: -Expert-Verified Answer An interactive combination of text, graphics, images, audio, video etc, is known as Multimedia. A collection of data in the form of text, pictures is known as Multimedia.

Concept note-3: -There are five basic elements of multimedia: text, images, audio, video and animation. Example-Text in fax, Photographic images, Geographic information system maps, Voice commands, Audio messages, Music, Graphics, Moving graphics animation, Full-motion stored and live video, Holographic images.

Concept note-4: -Multimedia refers to content that uses a combination of different content forms. It includes a combination of images, text, audio, video or interactivity content forms.

Concept note-5: -Multimedia is multiple forms of digital media, such as videos, images, audio, texts, etc. Animation refers to a type of multimedia that provides the viewers with an illusion of pictures moving in a sequential manner. Inter Relation. It is an integration/collection of various types of media.
