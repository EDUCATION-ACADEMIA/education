Errors in computer programmes are called:

(A) ** Bugs

(B) Spam

(C) Follies

(D) Mistakes

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Stemming from the first bug, today we call errors or glitches in a program a bug. Hopper was not present when the bug was found, but it became one of her favorite stories.

Concept note-2: -It was used by technicians before the emergence of computers. But still why a computer bug? Probably because in 1947, computer programmer Grace Hopper and her team found a bug – a real moth, lying in a relay of Harvard University’s Mark II electromechanical computer.

Concept note-3: -“In IT, a bug refers to an error, fault, or flaw in any computer program or a hardware system. A bug produces unexpected results or causes a system to behave unexpectedly. In short, it is any behavior or result that a program or system gets but it was not designed to do.”

Concept note-4: -“A mistake in coding is called Error, error found by tester is called Defect, defect accepted by development team then it is called Bug, build does not meet the requirements then it Is Failure.”
