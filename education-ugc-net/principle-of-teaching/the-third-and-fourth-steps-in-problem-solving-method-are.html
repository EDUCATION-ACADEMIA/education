The third and fourth steps in problem solving method are?

(A) Collection of data

(B) Evaluation

(C) ** Both A and B

(D) None of A and B

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Step 3: Carry out the plan (solve).

Concept note-2: -The “Four-Step Problem Solving” plan helps elementary math students to employ sound reasoning and to develop mathematical language while they complete a four-step problem-solving process. This problem-solving plan consists of four steps: details, main idea, strategy, and how.

Concept note-3: -The first two steps are for defining and measuring the problem. The third step is the analysis. And the fourth and fifth steps are improve and control, and address solutions.

Concept note-4: -Step One: Define the Problem. Step One is about diagnosing the problem – the context, background and symptoms of the issue. Step Two: Determine the Root Cause(s) of. Step Three: Develop Alternative Solutions. Step Four: Select a Solution. Step Five: Implement the Solution. Step Six: Evaluate the Outcome.
