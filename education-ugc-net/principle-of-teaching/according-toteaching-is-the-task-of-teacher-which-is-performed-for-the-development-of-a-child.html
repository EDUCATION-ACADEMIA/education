According to____”Teaching is the task of teacher which is performed for the development of a child.

(A) ** T.F Green

(B) Burton

(C) Brubacher

(D) None of them

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -According to Clarke, “Teaching refers to activities that are designed and performed to produced change in pupil behaviour.” 2. According to Thomas F. Green, “Teaching is the task of the teacher which is performed for the development of a child."

Concept note-2: -There is a very vital role of teachers in shaping the future of our children. They teach them values, morals, ethics, and discipline. In addition, they also guide students through their academic journey. They are the ones who inspire young minds to achieve great things.

Concept note-3: -Broadly speaking, the function of teachers is to help students learn by imparting knowledge to them and by setting up a situation in which students can and will learn effectively.

Concept note-4: -The Montessori Plus teaching method is a proven approach to early childhood education. It enables young learners to blossom as they understand more about themselves and the world around them. This foundation allows students to transition to formal schooling seamlessly and sets them up for long-term success.

Concept note-5: -The teacher, who builds their instruction on the fundamental ideas of holistic development, lets the student find and comprehend the various connections and correlations. The knowledge thus becomes a part of the child’s experience and becomes personal and socially significant.
