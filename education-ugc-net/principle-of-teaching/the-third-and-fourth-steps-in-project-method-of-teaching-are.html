The third and fourth steps in Project method of teaching are?

(A) Planning

(B) ** Execution

(C) Both A and B

(D) None of above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Therefore, the logical sequence of the Project method is Providing a situation ⇒ Choosing and purposing the Project ⇒ Planning of the Project ⇒ Executing the Project ⇒ Evaluation of the Project ⇒ Recording of the Project.

Concept note-2: -Kilpatrick devised four classes of projects for his method: construction (such as writing a play), enjoyment (such as experiencing a concert), problem (for instance, discussing a complex social problem like poverty), and specific learning (learning of skills such as swimming).

Concept note-3: -Hence, we conclude that there are four steps in a Project Method.

Concept note-4: -A project method is a medium of instruction where students are given a number of projects or situation. The students choose their situation on their own then they plan to execute the project collectively. In this style of teaching the teacher works as a guide and not a dictator but a working partner.

Concept note-5: -The fifth and final stage of the project method is evaluation. The whole work is reviewed by the teacher and students get judged or assessed on the basis of their performance. They carry out the project as planned, following which the mistakes they have made in the process are noted down.
