EDUCATION UGC NET

PRINCIPLE OF TEACHING

[SOURCES]
Syllabus of advance classes cannot be covered with ____,as it requires great time.

(A) Recitation

(B) Lecture method

(C) Discussion method

(D) ** Project method

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The project method is a teacher-facilitated collaborative approach in which students acquire and apply knowledge and skills to define and solve realistic problems using a process of extended inquiry. Projects are student-centered, following standards, parameters, and milestones clearly identified by the instructor.

Concept note-2: -Kilpatrick devised four classes of projects for his method: construction (such as writing a play), enjoyment (such as experiencing a concert), problem (for instance, discussing a complex social problem like poverty), and specific learning (learning of skills such as swimming).

Concept note-3: -Which of these is a merit of the teaching learning method known as project method? The experiences are hypothetical and hence, is an effective short-term learning method. It helps develop the discovery attitude.

Concept note-4: -Projects reinforce and stabilize interest, especially if it is the student who chooses the topic. The student wants to solve problems not imposed by the teacher. This is essential for self-activating processes within the learners and attributes to them a responsibility about the progress of their work.
