In Discussion method students gain ____

(A) Self-confidence

(B) Cooperation is learned

(C) Respect for other

(D) ** All of above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Students add to ideas, ask questions, and give examples to make the discussion deeper, longer, and more interesting.

Concept note-2: -Discussion methods are a variety of forums for open-ended, collaborative exchange of ideas among a teacher and students or among students for the purpose of furthering students thinking, learning, problem solving, understanding, or literary appreciation.

Concept note-3: -Discussion is important to learning in all disciplines because it helps students process information rather than simply receive it. Leading a discussion requires skills different from lecturing. The goal of a discussion is to get students to practice thinking about the course material.

Concept note-4: -A discussion can change your attitudes and ideas. A discussion can helps a group make a particular decision or come to a conclusion. A discussion gives you the chance to hear the thoughts and ideas of other students.
