(A) Simpson

(B) ** Burton

(C) Clarke

(D) Morrison

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -According to Burton, teaching is the stimulation, guidance, direction and encouragement of learning. It is the art of assisting discovery, not a mere lip service. It is the most respectful profession.

Concept note-2: -1. Ryburn’s view: “Teaching is a relationship which keeps the child to develop all his powers.” 2. Burton’s view: “Teaching is the stimulation guidance, direction and encouragement of learing.” Effective teaching: It can be defined in many ways including teacher behavior (warmth, clarity, etc)

Concept note-3: -Burton’s view: “Teaching is the stimulation guidance, direction and encouragement of learing.” 3. Smith’s view: In words of B.O. Smith, “Teaching is a system of actions intended to produce learning.”

Concept note-4: -B.O. Smith defined teaching as “Teaching is a system of actions intended to induce learning”. According to Gage (1963), “Teaching is a form interpersonal influence aimed at changing the behaviour potential another person”.

Concept note-5: -2.1 Definition of Teaching It means teaching process contains effective methods which develop students’ experience in learning process.
