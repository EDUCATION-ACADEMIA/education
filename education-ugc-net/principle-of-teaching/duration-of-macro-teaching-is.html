Duration of macro teaching is?

(A) ** 30 to 40 minutes

(B) 40 to 50 minutes

(C) 45 to 55 minutes

(D) 55 to 60 minutes

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A teacher delivers content for an extended period of time (usually an hour). The size of a class is usually 40 for school classrooms and may extend to a higher number if it’s a public lecture. It’s used by experienced teachers to introduce various skills to students throughout the academic year.

Concept note-2: -In the Indian model of micro-teaching, developed by NCERT the duration of Micro Teaching is as under: Teach-6 minutes. Feedback-6 minutes. Re-plan-12 minutes.

Concept note-3: -Define Micro Teaching Concept The standard time duration of a micro-teaching cycle is: Teach-6 minutes. Feedback-6 minutes. Re-plan-12 minutes.
