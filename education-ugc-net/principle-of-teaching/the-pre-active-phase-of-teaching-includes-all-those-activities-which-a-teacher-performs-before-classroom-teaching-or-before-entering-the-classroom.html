PRINCIPLE OF TEACHING

[SOURCES]
The pre active phase of teaching includes all those activities which a teacher performs before classroom teaching or before entering the classroom.

(A) After

(B) ** Before

(C) during

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer: Pre active phase of teaching is called Planning stage beacuse in this stage teacher plans everything that he will do in the teaching leaning process. For example the lesson, the methods of teaching, teaching learning materials etc.

Concept note-2: -In the pre-teaching phase, a teacher’s role is essentially that of a manager/pla er/decision maker. In this phase, teacher decides instructional objectives, ways and means of achieving these objectives, and ways and means of measuring the extent to which the objectives have been achieved.

Concept note-3: -Pre-Active Phase This phase is the planning phase of Teaching. Good planning helps a Teacher in smooth, functional, and successful task completion. Establishing certain goals or objectives and discovering ways to means to achieve these objectives.
