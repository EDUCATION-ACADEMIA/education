EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Teaching is the specialized application of knowledge, skills and attributes designed to provide unique service to meet the educational needs of the individual and of society. The choice of learning activities whereby the goals of education are realized in the school is the responsibility of the teaching profession.

Concept note-2: -1) Teaching is an interactive process: The main characteristic of teaching is that it is an interactive process. In the process of teaching, there are the interaction between teacher, student and the subject matter. With the help of this interaction that the learners to become active in any learning process.

Concept note-3: -Learning can be defined as acquiring knowledge, attitudes, orskills. Teaching is defined as helping another person to learn. Learning can be defined as acquiring knowledge, attitudes, orskills.

Concept note-4: -Instructor-Focused Teaching. Instructor-focused teaching-the approach to education that involves a central figure guiding the learning experience-is probably the most well-known teaching style. Student-Focused Teaching.
