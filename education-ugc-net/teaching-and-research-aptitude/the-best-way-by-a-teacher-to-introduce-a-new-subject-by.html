The best way by a teacher to introduce a new subject by

(A) Relating it to daily life situation

(B) Giving a broad outline of the subject

(C) Relating it to previously studied subject or course material

(D) ** Any of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The best way by a teacher to introduce a new subject by giving a broad outline of the subject, relating it to daily life situation and relating it to previously studied subject or course material.

Concept note-2: -Give your students some time to read or discuss new content as you introduce it. Ask the students to list what they already know about the content. This may include vocabulary words, places, people or ideas. You can use the items on the list as seeds to start a discussion on the topic.

Concept note-3: -Introducing Yourself as a Teacher Include your name (what you would like them to address you by), your academic background, and your interests. You could say something like, â€œGood morning class, my name is John Smith, you can call me John or Professor Smith.

Concept note-4: -Start with a Video. Everyone loves a good video, especially kids. Start with an Object. Another way to get your students wondering about a topic is to show them objects related to the content. Start with a Question. Start with Movement. Start with a Mistake. 14-Sept-2016

Concept note-5: -Attitude. Maintain a good attitude among your students to keep them engaged. Preparation. You should be familiar with the subject material. Organization. Prepare a lesson outline for what you want to deliver. Clarity. Activities. Patience. Unbiased Grading. 05-Jan-2022
