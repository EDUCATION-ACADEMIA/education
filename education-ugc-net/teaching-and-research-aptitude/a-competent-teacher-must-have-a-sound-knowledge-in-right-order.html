TEACHING AND RESEARCH APTITUDE

[SOURCES]
A competent teacher must have a sound knowledge in right order

(A) Theory, Research, Concept and Practice

(B) ** Concept, Theory, Practice and Research

(C) Research, Practice, Concept and Theory

(D) Practive, Concept, Theory and Research

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Solution(By Examveda Team) A competent teacher must have a sound knowledge in right order: Concept, theory, practice and research.

Concept note-2: -An understanding of learning theories can help educators to “reflect on their practice, improve upon, reshape and refine their work, and contribute to advancing the discipline” (Harasim, 2017).

Concept note-3: -People become teachers because they have a love for sharing knowledge and they want to instill confidence, self-worth, and a love of learning in them. This is even more true of special education teachers.

Concept note-4: -Teachers provide the power of education to today’s youth, thereby giving them the possibility for a better future. Teachers simplify the complex, and make abstract concepts accessible to students. Teachers also expose children to ideas and topics that they might otherwise not have come into contact with.
