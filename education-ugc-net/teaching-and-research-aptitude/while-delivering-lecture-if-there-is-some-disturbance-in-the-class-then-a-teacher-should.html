While delivering lecture if there is some disturbance in the class, then a teacher should

(A) Punish those causing disturbance.

(B) ** Keep quiet for a while and then go on.

(C) Not bother of what is happening in the class.

(D) All of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Take Off Quietly with Themes Elementary teachers can reduce noise by creating a theme in their classroom, like an airport. The “fasten your seatbelt” announcement is used to get children back to their seats and the dinging just before the captain speaks is used to gather the children’s attention.

Concept note-2: -Provide appropriate feedback: Learning occurs when students receive feedback on the performance of their assignments. All assignments need to be corrected and feedback should be given. This should occur as soon as possible after the assignments have been handed over.

Concept note-3: -Remember They’re Not Doing This on Purpose. Start with Non-Verbal Communication. Let Them Move at Their Own Pace. Use Positive Reinforcement. Learn Their Triggers. Avoid Labeling Them. Create Safe Spaces With Friends. Give Them a Job. More items •08-Jun-2022

Concept note-4: -report to the headmaster. report to the parents. punish the student appropriately. undertake a case study on the student. 02-Dec-2022
