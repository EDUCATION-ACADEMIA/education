The aim of vocationalization of education is :

(A) ** making liberal education job-oriented

(B) converting liberal education into vocational education

(C) preparing students for a vocation along with knowledge

(D) giving more importance to vocational than general education

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hence, The aim of vocationalisation of Education is to prepare students for a vocation along with regular general education.

Concept note-2: -According to the recommendation of the Secondary Education Commission (1952-53). The aim of the vocationalisation of education is to improve the vocational efficiency of the students.

Concept note-3: -Vocationalisation of education is designed to introduce manual skills in general education. Vocationalisation of education means training in some vocations at the secondary, Higher Secondary level with general education.

Concept note-4: -Vocational education is a different learning option from the path of college or universities. It offers a path that imparts the skills and knowledge needed to work in a given occupation. It is coordinated with the requirements of the labor market and is an integral part of the education system.
