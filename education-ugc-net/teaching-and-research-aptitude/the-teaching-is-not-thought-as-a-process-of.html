TEACHING AND RESEARCH APTITUDE

[SOURCES]
The teaching is not thought as a process of

(A) ** Indoctrinating adult ideas to young people

(B) Listening the recitation of pupils

(C) Directing the activities of people

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Teaching is not a process of giving instructions. It facilitates learning by using constructive instructional methods.

Concept note-2: -Teaching is the ability to assist learners in orga-nizing, directing, and maximizing the stream of developing life experiences. As Dewey stated, teaching relies upon “the educational significance of social arrangements [as] means used to educate the young” (1916/Dewey 1997a, p. 89).

Concept note-3: -Teaching and learning process can be defined as a transformation process of knowledge from teachers to students.

Concept note-4: -The purpose and importance of teaching and learning materials is to make lessons interesting, learning easy and enable teachers to easily express concepts. Learning materials can significantly increase learners’ achievement by supporting learning.
