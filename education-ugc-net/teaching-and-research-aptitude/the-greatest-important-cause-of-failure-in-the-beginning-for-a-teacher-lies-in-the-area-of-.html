The greatest important Cause Of Failure In The Beginning For A Teacher Lies In The Area Of :

(A) Verbal ability

(B) ** Interpersonal relationship

(C) Knowledge of the teacher

(D) Light-handling of the student

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Thus, it is clear that the greatest important cause of failure in beginning for a teacher lies in the area of his / her communicative ability.

Concept note-2: -Lack of persistence More people fail not because they lack knowledge or talent but because they just quit. It’s important to remember two words: persistence and resistance. Persist in what must be done and resist what ought not to be done.

Concept note-3: -School failure can occur as a result of emotional issues, mental health problems, learning disorders, and inability to meet the standards the school sets.

Concept note-4: -Hence, Personality and its ability to relate to the class and to the pupils is the most important single factor in underlying the success of being a teacher.

Concept note-5: -Pre-Planning. There cannot be any job in the world that yields equal rewards and frustrations as the first year of teaching. Time Management and Routines. Engaging Students. Attendance. Parent Communication. New Student Needs. Ask for Help. Plan and Schedule. More items •25-Apr-2022
