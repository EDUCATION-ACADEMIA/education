Some students are weak in studies. What will be your attitude towards them?

(A) ** Strict

(B) Liberal

(C) Lenient

(D) Lovable

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -You will have strict attitude towards them.

Concept note-2: -Answer: A student should have a positive attitude towards studies. Don’t mind what others say because they may say that you wont be able to do it but if you have that mindset where you think you can do it then you will prove the others wrong and you will become the best in your studies.

Concept note-3: -They might either be busy in talking among their friends or daydreaming in the class. As a result, they miss out on the key points that later become their weaknesses. Another habit that makes students weak in studies is that either they do not make notes or the notes that they make are inappropriate.

Concept note-4: -Analyze Why and Where Are They Lacking In Studies? Concise Lessons With Demonstrated Examples. Focus On Encouragement And Motivation. Small Group Discussions And Learning. Effective Learning – Flowcharts. Healthy Feedback.

Concept note-5: -Give students a sense of control. Be clear about learning objectives. Create a threat-free environment. Change your scenery. Offer varied experiences. Use positive competition. Offer rewards. Give students responsibility. More items •11-Dec-2012
