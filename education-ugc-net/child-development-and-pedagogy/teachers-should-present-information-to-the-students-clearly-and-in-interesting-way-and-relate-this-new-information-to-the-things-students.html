CHILD DEVELOPMENT AND PEDAGOGY

[SOURCES]
Teachers should present information to the students clearly and in interesting way, and relate this new information to the things students

(A) don’t know

(B) ** already know

(C) willing to know

(D) not willing to know

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Positive teacher-student relationships help students meet these needs. Teachers offer feedback to students to support their feelings of competence. Teachers who know their students’ interests and preferences, and show regard and respect for these individual differences, bolster students’ feelings of autonomy.

Concept note-2: -Communication is the key to Know About Students Interpersonal communication and classroom management skills are also an important part of it. When teachers and students build a relationship, it’s the most important thing they can do to help them get to know each other.

Concept note-3: -Whenever possible, include components that connect the content to previously learned material. Provide time for students to ask questions, identify what they already know and make connections to their personal interests. Create a series of statements that are related to new content you are preparing to teach.

Concept note-4: -Develop A Reading Atmosphere: Put The Child In Charge As Much As Possible: Encourage Open And Sincere Communication: Focus On Your Child’s Interest: Encourage Different Types Of Learning Styles: Share Your Enthusiasm For Learning: Game-Based Learning Is The Key: More items
