CHILD DEVELOPMENT AND PEDAGOGY

Question [CLICK ON ANY CHOICE TO KNOW THE RIGHT ANSWER]
A child has been admitted to your school who belongs to a backward family/background from the cultural viewpoint You will.
A
Keep him in a class in which there are many more students of backward background from the cultural viewpoint
B
Send a teacher to know more about the backward cultural background of the child
C
Keep him in a normal class but will make special arrangements for teaching him keeping his special needs in view
D
Advise him to take up vocational education
Explanation: 

Detailed explanation-1: -Method of Teaching Backward Children A healthy atmosphere should be created at school and also in the community. It should be conducive to learning. Teaching should be arranged according to the interests of these children. Their needs and problems should be considered.

Detailed explanation-2: -A child is called backward when his educational achievement is below the level of his natural abilities. The cause can be ranging from many physical, mental, and personal conditions. Defective eyesight, hearing, speech defects, or chronic disease or illness are some physical causes.

Detailed explanation-3: -Talk with Your Pediatrician A child’s lack of academic progress is often a symptom of more complex issues such as various types and combinations of behavioral, psychological, and learning difficulties. Social stress, illness, and chronic medical problems may also play a role.

Detailed explanation-4: -Remedial teaching is used for the students who are in general backward in studies and fail in one subject as the objective of the remedial method is to give additional help to learners who have fallen behind the rest of the class in any topic or subject.
