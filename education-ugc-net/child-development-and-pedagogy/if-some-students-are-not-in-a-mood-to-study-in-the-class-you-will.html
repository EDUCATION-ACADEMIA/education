If some students are not in a mood to study in the class you will.

(A) Force them to study

(B) Tell those students to leave the class and enjoy

(C) Warn them that they must study else you will report the matter to the Principal

(D) ** Tell them some interesting things related to their interests or your own subject

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Reasons why students lose interest in studies Difficult syllabus: Several schools have a rigid curriculum and students are subjected to repeated tests or heavy assignments. Some children find it difficult to cope with the increasing demands of school work and to give up under such pressure.

Concept note-2: -Acceptance Before Expectations. Look Out Of Their Interests. Encourage Their Curiosity. De-Emphasize School. Make Studying Enjoyable. Learning Together. Minimize distractions. Stay Positive. More items •08-Nov-2020

Concept note-3: -Don’t procrastinate. Waiting until a few days, or the day before the test can make the task seem too big. Acknowledge why you don’t want to study. Knowing the cause of a problem is the key to finding the solution. Study in a tidy place. Set limits. Get competitive. 21-Oct-2020

Concept note-4: -Write a song. Reward yourself with kittens. Think on the move. Make a recording. Turn your doodles into study notes. Pursue lots of hobbies.
