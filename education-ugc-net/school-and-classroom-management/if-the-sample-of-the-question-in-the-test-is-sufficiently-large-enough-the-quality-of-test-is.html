If the sample of the question in the test is sufficiently large enough, the quality of test is?

(A) ** Adequacy

(B) Reliability

(C) Objectivity

(D) Differentiability

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Reliability is the degree to which an assessment tool produces stable and consistent results. Test-retest reliability is a measure of reliability obtained by administering the same test twice over a period of time to a group of individuals.

Concept note-2: -Probably you can determine quality of test questions by determining their level of difficulty, discriminatory, fairness, validity, reliability, objectivity and clarity.

Concept note-3: -Objective Type Tests are highly reliable and valid as they require one-word answer thus minimize the subjective inference and judgment.

Concept note-4: -Validity refers to the degree to which a test measures what it is supposed to measure.
