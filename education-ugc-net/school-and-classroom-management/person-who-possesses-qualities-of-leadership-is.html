Person who possesses qualities of leadership is

(A) ** Leader

(B) Officer

(C) Manager

(D) Administrator

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A good leader should have integrity, self-awareness, courage, respect, empathy, and gratitude. They should be learning agile and flex their influence while communicating and delegating effectively. See how these key leadership qualities can be learned and improved at all levels of your organization.

Concept note-2: -Good leaders possess self-awareness, garner credibility, focus on relationship-building, have a bias for action, exhibit humility, empower others, stay authentic, present themselves as constant and consistent, become role models and are fully present.

Concept note-3: -A leader is someone who directs and motivates a group of people or an individual to accomplish a certain goal. Those who are leaders inspire followers to take the initiatives necessary for success. The abilities required to be an effective leader must be developed via learning and practice.

Concept note-4: -Leadership Personality Traits: Key Takeaway If you want to be an effective leader, you need to be kind, thoughtful, creative, an effective communicator, self-aware, have integrity and empathy, engaged, passionate, ethical, and accountable.

Concept note-5: -A leader is someone who inspires passion and motivation in followers. A leader is someone with a vision and the path to realizing it. A leader is someone who ensures their team has support and tools to achieve their goals.
