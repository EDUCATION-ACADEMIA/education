Test item cannot discriminate low achievers and high achievers when its value is lower than?

(A) ** 0.30

(B) 1

(C) 0.30 - 1

(D) None

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Results showed high achievers experience less test anxiety as compared to low achievers. Female high achievers experienced more test anxiety as compared to male high achievers whereas male low achievers experienced more test anxiety than female low achievers.

Concept note-2: -High achievers (HA) were classified as learners who obtained 118 correct answers or above on the achievement test; in contrast, low achievers (LA) were learners who obtained 89 correct answers or below on the achievement test.

Concept note-3: -High achievers seek out challenges. They strive to meet them, motivated by an attitude that success is good to be proud of. In contrast, low achievers are challenge-avoidant. They tend towards worrying about failure, which can cause them to not take action when needed.

Concept note-4: -: an unsuccessful person who does not work hard.
