Limited to quantitative description of pupil’s performance is?

(A) Test

(B) Evaluation

(C) Assessment

(D) ** Measurement

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Limited to the quantitative description of the pupil’s performance is? answer choices. Test. Measurement. Assessment.

Concept note-2: -Hence, it could be concluded that evaluation is both quantitative and qualitative.

Concept note-3: -What are Norm-referenced tests? These test measure student’s performance in comparison to other students. Also, the age and question paper is same for both of them. They measure whether the students have performed better or worse than other test takers.

Concept note-4: -Measurement.-is a process of obtaining a numerical description of the degree to which an individual possesses a particular characteristic.
