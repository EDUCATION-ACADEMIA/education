(A) May 7th 1731

(B) ** May 5th 1731

Concept note-1: -Deoband School The Tablighi Jamaat (literal meaning the Missionary Organization) was founded by Deoband ulema with the aim of spreading Islam all over the world.

Concept note-2: -It was established in the North Indian town of Deoband, Uttar Pradesh, in 1867 by a group of prominent Indian Muslim scholars (’Ulama’). More specifically, the charismatic scholars Rashid Ahmad Gangohi (d. 1906) and Qasim Nanautvi (d.

Concept note-3: -Shah Walliullah was a great Muslim reformist of 18th century in India. He was a brilliant thinker and scholar with critical insight of political scenario of that time. He worked for the betterment and true education of Muslims on right Islamic norms.

Concept note-4: -Shāh Walī Allāh Walī Allāh received a traditional Islamic education from his father and is said to have memorized the Qurʾān at the age of seven.
