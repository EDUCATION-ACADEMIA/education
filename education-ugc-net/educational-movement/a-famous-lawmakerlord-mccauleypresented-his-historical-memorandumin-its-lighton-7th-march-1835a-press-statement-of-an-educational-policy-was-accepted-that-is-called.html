EDUCATIONAL MOVEMENT

[SOURCES]
A famous lawmaker,Lord McCauley,Presented his historical memorandum.In its light,on 7th March 1835,a press statement of an educational policy was accepted that is called____

(A) Western Resolution

(B) McCauley,s Resolution

(C) ** Bentinck,s Resolution

(D) Educational Resolution

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In his minute on education, he justified the use of English as the medium of instruction, and also the teaching of western education to Indians.

Concept note-2: -The Bentinck Policy made the resolution of Macaulay’s views on the stress of English language as a medium of instruction. In the orders of Bentinck policy, the whole focus was given to invest all the expenditure of education in improvement of English literature and science only.

Concept note-3: -He appointed a committee headed by Lord Macaulay to make recommendations for the promotion of education. In his report, Macaulay emphasized the promotion of European literature and science through English medium to the people of India. This recommendation was wholeheartedly accepted by William Bentinck.

Concept note-4: -Macaulay recommended 1. English shoud be the medium of instruction in schools 2. Indians should be taught sciences and western literature. He had a very poor opinion of traditional Indian learning which he thought was inferior to European learning.
