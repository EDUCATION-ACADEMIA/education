Shibli was a supporter of the ____and was critical of Sir Syed,s attitude towards it.

(A) Muslim League

(B) Azamghar

(C) ** Congress

(D) Deo Band

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -He set up many educational institutes to propagate education, the most significant being the Muhammadan Anglo-Oriental College (MAOC) which he set up in 1875. This later became the Aligarh Muslim University. The most bitter critic of Sir Syed Ahmed Khan was Akbar Ilahabadi.

Concept note-2: -He is known for the founding of the Shibli National College in 1883 and the Darul Mussanifin (House of Writers) in Azamgarh. As a supporter of the Deobandi school, he favoured the inclusion of English Language and European sciences in the system of education.
