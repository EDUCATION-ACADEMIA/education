Edinburgh University granted Sir Syed the honorary degree of____in 1889.

(A) Ph.D

(B) ** Doctorate

(C) Masters

(D) Post Graduate

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -He is regarded as one of the founders of the Two-Nation Theory which says that Hindus and Muslims cannot be one nation. Sir Syed was knighted by the British in 1888.

Concept note-2: -In 1875, Sir Syed founded the Madarsatul Uloom in Aligarh and patterned the MAO College after Oxford and Cambridge universities that he went on a trip to London. His objective was to build a college in line with the British education system but without compromising its Islamic values.

Concept note-3: -Syed Ahmed Khan received a knighthood in 1888 for his His loyalty. He wanted to reconcile western scientific education with the teachings of the Quran. He served the British administration before the revolt of 1857.

Concept note-4: -In 1859, Syed established Gulshan School at Muradabad, Victoria School at Ghazipur in 1863, and a scientific society for Muslims in 1863. In 1875, founded the Muhammadan Anglo-Oriental College, the first Muslim university in Southern Asia.
