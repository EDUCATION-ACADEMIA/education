The Muslims of that time were suffering from lethargy and ____

(A) Conformism

(B) ** Conservatism

(C) Activism

(D) Extremism

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -✓ Muslims believe it is inevitable to sometimes cause suffering to others. They believe it is important to be honest with themselves, and to repair the damage they have done. ✓ They believe God will forgive anyone who sincerely repents. ‘Be compassionate towards the destitute’.

Concept note-2: -Muslims attribute the moral quality of suffering (the badness or evil thereof) only to human action. Everything else is under God’s direct control and therefore, despite all delusions to the contrary, perfect in design. Physical pain and ailment are common to human life.

Concept note-3: -Wahhabism advocates a purification of Islam, rejects Islamic theology and philosophy developed after the death of the Prophet Muhammad, and calls for strict adherence to the letter of the Koran and hadith [the recorded sayings and practices of the Prophet].
