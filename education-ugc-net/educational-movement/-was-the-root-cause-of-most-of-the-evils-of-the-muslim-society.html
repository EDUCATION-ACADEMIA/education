____ was the root cause of most of the evils of the Muslim society.

(A) Lethargy

(B) Idleness

(C) ** lliteracy

(D) Fatalism

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Poverty-This is the root cause of Illiteracy . There are still people who do not have access to proper food, shelter and clothing . They can not afford to even send their children to school and are forced to put them to work may be in the farming activities or any other form of work they are in.

Concept note-2: -Economic disparities, gender discrimination, caste discrimination, and technological barriers lead to illiteracy in India. India has the largest population of illiterate adults, which further contribute to this vicious cycle of illiteracy in India.

Concept note-3: -The Social Impact A person who cannot read and write struggles to know their rights, to vote, to find work, to pay bills and to secure housing. They also may have low self-esteem or feel emotions such as shame, fear and powerlessness, which can often lead to isolation.

Concept note-4: -Fact: According to the census 2021 data, literacy rates of Muslims is 68.5% and Christians is 84.5%.
