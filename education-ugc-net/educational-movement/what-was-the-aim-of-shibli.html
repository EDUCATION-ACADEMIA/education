[SOURCES]
What was the aim of Shibli?

(A) To follow Deo Band

(B) To follow Congress

(C) To promote Aligarh

(D) ** To bridge the gulf between Aligarh and Deo Band

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Both Deoband and Aligarh were educational movements that aimed at providing basic education to the Muslims of Subcontinent. Both movements rendered great services in the education and uplift of Muslims of India. They achieved what they were built for.

Concept note-2: -Deoband Movement was started in Deoband in Saharanpur district in Uttar Pradesh. It was launched in 1866 by Mohammad Qasim Nanotavi and Rashid Ahmed Gangohi. Objective: To abolish British rule and restore the Muslim Community by educating them. It was initiated by the orthodox section among the Muslim community.

Concept note-3: -Nadwatul Ulama was formed with the aim to bring all the sects of Islam together irrespective of some of their differences in beliefs. Nadwa means assembly and group, it was named so because it was constituted by a group of Indian Islamic scholars from different theological schools.
