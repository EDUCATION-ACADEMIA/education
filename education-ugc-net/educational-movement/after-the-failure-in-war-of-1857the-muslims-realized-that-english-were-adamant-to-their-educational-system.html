After the failure in war of 1857,the Muslims realized that English were adamant to ____their educational system.

(A) Promote

(B) Nullify

(C) ** Abolish

(D) Establish

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -After 1857, the British established their rule in which the Muslims were removed from the government services as Hunter writes: The exclusion of the Muslims from army and the higher posts of administration were necessary for the safety of the British rule; but this policy ruined the Muslims.

Concept note-2: -Complete answer: The British treat Muslims badly after the 1857 rebellion because they accuse them of being involved in the Revolt of 1857, which was organised as an all-India organisation to form a broad-based liberation campaign.

Concept note-3: -Answer. The post 1857 period posed many challenges which were multifarious in nature including the loss of political power, the ruin of their economic bases and a threat to the annihilation of Islamic culture both material and non material. Among these the attacks on Islam by certain orientalists were the most serious.
