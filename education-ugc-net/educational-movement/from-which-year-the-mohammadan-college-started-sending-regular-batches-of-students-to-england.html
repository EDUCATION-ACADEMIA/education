From which year the Mohammadan College started sending regular batches of students to England?

(A) 1882

(B) 1881

(C) 1888

(D) ** 1880

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sir Sayyid Ahmad Khan founded the Mohammedan Anglo-Oriental College at Aligarh in 1875. Later, this college grew into the famous Aligarh Muslim University. Q.

Concept note-2: -It was established as Madrasatul Uloom Musalmanan-e-Hind in 1875, and after two years it became Muhammadan Anglo-Oriental College. The statesman Syed Ahmad Khan founded the predecessor of Aligarh Muslim University, the Muhammadan Anglo Oriental College, in 1875 having already established two schools.

Concept note-3: -On 9th September 1920 the Mohammedan Anglo-Oriental College (MAO College) of Aligarh was converted into the Aligarh Muslim University (AMU).

Concept note-4: -Sir Syed Ahmad Khan established the Muhammadan Anglo-Oriental College in Aligarh in 1875. It was open to students from all religions.
