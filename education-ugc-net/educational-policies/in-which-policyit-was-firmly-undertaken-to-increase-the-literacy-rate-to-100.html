[SOURCES]
In which policy,it was firmly undertaken to increase the literacy rate to 100%?

(A) The Educational Policy 1959

(B) The Educational Policy 1972

(C) ** The Educational Policy 1992

(D) The Educational Policy 1998

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Programme of Action (PoA) 1992, under the National Policy on Education (NPE), 1986 envisaged to conduct of a common entrance examination on all India basis for admission to professional and technical programmes in the country.

Concept note-2: -The main objective of the National Policy of Education of 1986 and Programme of Action, 1992 was to establish a national system of education implies that all students irrespective of caste; creed, sex, and religion have access to education of a comparable quality.

Concept note-3: -Plan of Action 1992 acknowledged the need to further expand opportunities for secondary education. Like other education policies in India, the NPE had also envisioned better participation of the backward classes. However, the POA 1992 increased the autonomy of the Boards of Secondary Education and reorganized them.

Concept note-4: -In 2002, through the Eighty Sixth Constitutional Amendment Act 2002, Right to Education was inserted as Article 21 A (Fundamental Rights) in the Constitution of India to provide free and compulsory education to children of age six to fourteen.
