What would be the proportion of education of Science and Arts according to the Educational Policy 1998?

(A) 40:60

(B) ** 50:50

(C) 30:70

(D) 20:80

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -According to NEP 2020, by 2025, at least 50% of learners shall have vocational exposure through school and higher education. Every child is supposed to learn at least one vocation and be exposed to several more.

Concept note-2: -The new policy targets a 100% Gross Enrolment Ratio (GER) in school education in next 10 years (by the year 2030) and also envisions universalization of education from pre-school to secondary level.

Concept note-3: -Under this structure, the first five years of schooling will be focused on foundational learning, followed by three years of preparatory schooling, three years of middle stage schooling, and four years of secondary education.

Concept note-4: -The new policy aims for a 100 percent Gross Enrollment Ratio (GER) in school education within the next ten years (by 2030), as well as the universalization of education from pre-school to secondary level.
