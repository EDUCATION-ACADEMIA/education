What degree was introduced to the National Institute of Science and Technology?

(A) ** B.ED

(B) MA

(C) MSC

(D) PhD

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A B. Ed or Bachelor of Education is a 2-year professional degree specially meant for those wishing to pursue a career in teaching. Basic eligibility for B. Ed is an undergraduate degree (BA, B.Com or B.Sc) in any discipline from a recognised university with an aggregate of 50%.

Concept note-2: -B. Ed is a Bachelor degree where as M.Sc is a Masters course. After finishing the +2, we can do B Ed where as Msc is done after finishing the graduation, But there are also dual degree courses(integrated courses) where you can do both BEd and MSc together.

Concept note-3: -A Bachelor of Education (B. Ed.) is an undergraduate professional degree which prepares students for work as a teacher in schools.

Concept note-4: -Radio in the 1920s sparked an entirely new wave of learning; on-air classes began popping up for any student within listening range. Next came the overhead projector in 1930, followed by the ballpoint pen in 1940 and headphones in 1950.
