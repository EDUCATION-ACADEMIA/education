To whom,the Additional Secretaries give help?

(A) Provincial Educational Minster

(B) Secretary of Education

(C) Federal Education Minster

(D) ** Deputy Secretary

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -An additional secretary in charge of administration also exercises all administrative powers as head of the department wing of the ministry/department. Additional secretaries and joint secretaries are responsible for filing all affidavits and responses before the Supreme Court of India.

Concept note-2: -In the Hierarchy of a Ministry in the Government of India, the position of Joint Secretary is below Additional Secretary and above Directors and Deputy Secretaries.

Concept note-3: -(C) Director/Deputy Secretary and Equivalent Officers– Director /Deputy Secretary is an officer who acts on behalf of the Secretary. He holds charge of a Secretariat Division and is responsible for the disposal of Government business dealt within the Division under his charge.
