In the Education Policy 1978-80 which saying of the holy Prophet was made the basis for the adult education?
A
To get knowledge is the duty of every Muslim man and woman
B
Get knowledge from cradle to the grave
C
Both A and B
D
None of the above mentioned
Explanation: 

Detailed explanation-1: -Whosoever follows a path to seek knowledge therein, God will make easy for him a path to paradise.

Detailed explanation-2: -The Quran is the central religious text of Islam. It represents the fountainhead of Divine guidance for every Muslim. The Islamic sacred book is the words of God and was dictated to Muhammad, the prophet of Islam, by the Archangel Gabriel and was written down in Arabic.

Detailed explanation-3: -And the belief system that arose from Muhammad’s ideas became the basis of one of the world’s most widely practiced religions: Islam. Muhammad was born around 570 in the city of Mecca, located on the Arabian Peninsula.

Detailed explanation-4: -The rise of Islam is intrinsically linked with the Prophet Muhammad, believed by Muslims to be the last in a long line of prophets that includes Moses and Jesus.
