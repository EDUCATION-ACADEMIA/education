What would promote the programmes of PTC and CT for teacher’s training?

(A) Provincial Government

(B) Central Government

(C) Federal Government

(D) ** Allama Iqbal Open University

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Allama Iqbal Open University (AIOU) plays a significant role in the training of future educators in Pakistan via remote education. Educator preparation at a distance and orders are both available at Pakistan’s largest institution, Allama Iqbal Open University.

Concept note-2: -The Distance Education Council (DEC) was established in 1991 under Section 16(7) read with Section 5(2) of the IGNOU Act, 1985. As per clause (2) (a) of the statute 28, the DEC was responsible for the promotion and coordination of the Open and Distance Learning system in the Country.

Concept note-3: -Television has proved more useful in distance education by bringing academic material to the students in a more direct and personal way as compare to the radio and printed material. Moreover, it gives a sense of association to the students who are widely scattered.

Concept note-4: -Policies & Procedures. Teaching Methodologies. Communication Skills. Leadership & Management.
